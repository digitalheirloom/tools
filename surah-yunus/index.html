Surah Yunus

(Chapter 10: Jonah) - Verses 1-109 Analysis 📖

📜 Introduction: Setting the Scene

  • Place and Context of Revelation

    • Historical Setting: Revealed in Mecca (circa 610-618 CE), during the early to middle Meccan period when Muslims faced intense persecution from the Quraysh. Some scholars suggest a few verses may have Medinan context due to their tone.
    • Geographical Context: Mecca, the heart of Arabian polytheism, where the Prophet Muhammad preached monotheism amidst hostility and rejection.
    • Circumstances: Named after Prophet Yunus (Jonah), mentioned in verse 98, this surah addresses the struggles of prophethood, the rejection of divine messages, and the consequences of disbelief. It emphasizes Allah’s oneness (Tawhid), His signs in creation, and the inevitability of divine judgment. The surah comforts the Prophet and believers facing opposition, drawing parallels with past prophets like Noah, Moses, and Jonah.

  • 🔑 Key Themes and Topics

    • ☝️ Tawhid and Divine Signs: Affirms Allah’s unity and highlights His signs in nature and history.
    • 📜 Prophethood and Rejection: Explores the challenges prophets face and disbelievers’ denial.
    • ⚖️ Divine Justice: Warns of accountability on the Day of Judgment.
    • 🕊️ Mercy and Guidance: Encourages repentance and trust in Allah’s mercy.
    • 🌍 Human Responsibility: Urges reflection on creation and acceptance of truth.

  • 🌍 Relevance Today

    • ⏳ Facing Rejection: Offers solace for those facing opposition in upholding truth, relevant to personal and communal struggles.
    • 🌟 Reflection on Nature: Encourages appreciating the universe’s order, aligning with modern scientific curiosity.
    • ⚖️ Accountability: Reminds of personal responsibility, applicable to ethical and moral discussions.
    • 🙏 Hope in Mercy: Promotes repentance and optimism, resonating across spiritual traditions.

10:4إِلَيْهِ مَرْجِعُكُمْ جَمِيعًا...Usi ki taraf tum sab ki baazi hai...To Him is your return, all of you...⚖️ Day of Return: Like Christian judgment day (Revelation 20:12). All will face Allah’s justice.
10:5هُوَ الَّذِي جَعَلَ الشَّمْسَ ضِيَاءً...Wahi hai jisne sooraj ko roshni aur chand ko noor banaya...It is He Who made the sun a shining light and the moon a derived light...🌞 Celestial Signs: Like Hindu cosmic order (Rigveda 10.85). Sun and moon reflect divine precision.
10:6إِنَّ فِي اخْتِلَافِ اللَّيْلِ وَالنَّهَارِ...Raat aur din ke tabadle mein nishaniyan hain...In the alternation of night and day are signs...🌍 Nature’s Signs: Like Jewish signs in creation (Psalm 104:19). Natural cycles point to Allah’s power.
10:7إِنَّ الَّذِينَ لَا يَرْجُونَ لِقَاءَنَا...Jo hamari mulaqat ki umeed nahi rakhte...Those who do not expect to meet Us...🚫 Denying Hereafter: Like Christian rejecting eternity (1 Corinthians 15:19). Disbelievers focus on worldly life.
10:8أُولَٰئِكَ مَأْوَاهُمُ النَّارُ...Unka thikana aag hai...Their abode is the Fire...🔥 Punishment for Denial: Like Jewish fate of wicked (Isaiah 66:24). Disbelievers face hellfire.
10:9إِنَّ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ...Jo imaan laaye aur neik kaam kiye...Those who believe and do righteous deeds...🌴 Guided to Paradise: Like Christian path to salvation (John 3:16). Believers guided to blissful rivers.
10:10دَعْوَاهُمْ فِيهَا سُبْحَانَكَ اللَّهُمَّ...Unka pukarna wahan “Subhanak Allahumma” hoga...Their call therein will be, “Glory be to You, O Allah...”🙏 Paradise’s Praise: Like Jewish heavenly worship (Psalm 148:2). Believers glorify Allah in paradise.
10:11وَلَوْ يُعَجِّلُ اللَّهُ لِلنَّاسِ الشَّرَّ...Agar Allah logon ke liye buraai jaldi karta...If Allah hastened evil for people as they seek good...⚖️ Divine Patience: Like Christian God’s forbearance (2 Peter 3:9). Allah delays punishment for reflection.
10:12وَإِذَا مَسَّ الْإِنْسَانَ الضُّرُّ دَعَانَا...Jab insaan ko takleef chhoti hai, woh hamein pukarta hai...When harm touches man, he calls upon Us...🙏 Crisis Prayer: Like Jewish cries in distress (Psalm 107:13). Humans turn to God in trouble, forget in ease.
10:13وَلَقَدْ أَهْلَكْنَا الْقُرُونَ مِنْ قَبْلِكُمْ...Humne tum se pehle kayi naslon ko halaak kiya...We destroyed generations before you...⚡ Past Destruction: Like Christian flood judgment (2 Peter 2:5). Warns of consequences for rejecting messengers.
10:14ثُمَّ جَعَلْنَاكُمْ خَلَائِفَ فِي الْأَرْضِ...Phir humne tumhein zameen mein khalifa banaya...Then We made you successors on earth...🌍 Human Responsibility: Like Jewish stewardship (Genesis 2:15). Humans tested as earth’s caretakers.
10:15وَإِذَا تُتْلَىٰ عَلَيْهِمْ آيَاتُنَا بَيِّنَاتٍ...Jab hamari wazeh aayatein un par padhi jati hain...When Our clear verses are recited to them...🚫 Demanding New Quran: Like Jewish rejecting prophets (Jeremiah 44:16). Disbelievers demand altered revelation.
10:16قُلْ لَوْ شَاءَ اللَّهُ مَا تَلَوْتُهُ عَلَيْكُمْ...Kaho, “Agar Allah chahta toh main yeh tumhein na padhta...”Say, “If Allah had willed, I would not have recited it to you...”🌟 Divine Will: Like Christian God’s plan (Acts 2:23). Prophet’s mission is Allah’s command, not his choice.
10:17فَمَنْ أَظْلَمُ مِنْ مَنِ افْتَرَىٰ عَلَى اللَّهِ...Us se zyada zalim kaun jo Allah par jhoot bandhe...Who is more unjust than one who invents a lie against Allah...🚫 Falsehood Condemned: Like Jewish false prophets (Deuteronomy 18:20). Lying about God or denying Quran is grave.
10:18وَيَعْبُدُونَ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ مَا لَا يَضُرُّهُمْ...Woh Allah ke siwa uski ibadat karte hain jo na nuqsaan de sakta hai...They worship besides Allah what neither harms nor benefits...🚫 Idolatry Rebuked: Like Hindu critique of lifeless idols (Bhagavad Gita 12:5). Idols powerless compared to Allah.
10:19وَمَا كَانَ النَّاسُ إِلَّا أُمَّةً وَاحِدَةً...Log ek hi ummat the, phir unhone ikhtilaaf kiya...Mankind was but one nation, then they differed...😔 Human Division: Like Christian unity lost (1 Corinthians 1:10). Humanity split after original monotheism.
10:20وَيَقُولُونَ لَوْلَا أُنْزِلَ عَلَيْهِ آيَةٌ...Woh kehte hain, “Us par koi nishani kyun nahi utari...”They say, “Why has no sign been sent down to him...”❓ Demanding Signs: Like Jewish seeking miracles (Exodus 17:7). Disbelievers challenge Prophet for proofs.
10:21وَإِذَا أَذَقْنَا النَّاسَ رَحْمَةً مِنْ بَعْدِ ضَرَّاءَ...Jab hum logon ko takleef ke baad rehmat chakhate hain...When We let people taste mercy after adversity...😈 Forgetting Mercy: Like Christian ungrateful servants (Luke 17:17). People plot after relief.
10:22هُوَ الَّذِي يُسَيِّرُكُمْ فِي الْبَرِّ وَالْبَحْرِ...Wahi hai jo tumhein khushki aur samandar mein chalta hai...It is He Who enables you to travel on land and sea...🌊 Storm Prayer: Like Jewish sea rescue (Psalm 107:28). People call Allah in peril, forget in safety.
10:23فَلَمَّا أَنْجَاهُمْ إِذَا هُمْ يَبْغُونَ فِي الْأَرْضِ...Jab humne unhein bacha liya, toh woh zameen mein baghaawat karte hain...But when He saves them, they rebel on earth...🚫 Ingratitude: Like Christian returning to sin (2 Peter 2:22). Humans resume wrongdoing after salvation.
10:24إِنَّمَا مَثَلُ الْحَيَاةِ الدُّنْيَا كَمَاءٍ...Dunya ki zindagi ki misaal pani jaisi hai...The example of worldly life is like rain...🌱 Worldly Life’s Transience: Like Hindu fleeting maya (Bhagavad Gita 8:15). Worldly gains are temporary.
10:25وَاللَّهُ يَدْعُو إِلَىٰ دَارِ السَّلَامِ...Allah aman ke ghar ki taraf bulata hai...Allah calls to the Home of Peace...🌴 Paradise’s Call: Like Christian eternal rest (Hebrews 4:9). Allah guides to peace and paradise.
10:26لِلَّذِينَ أَحْسَنُوا الْحُسْنَىٰ وَزِيَادَةٌ...Jo neik kaam karte hain, unke liye behtareen aur zyada hai...For those who do good is the best and more...🌟 Reward for Good: Like Jewish blessings for righteousness (Deuteronomy 28:2). Faithful gain extra rewards.
10:27وَالَّذِينَ كَسَبُوا السَّيِّئَاتِ جَزَاءُ سَيِّئَةٍ...Jinhone buraai ki, unhein buraai ka badla milega...Those who earn evil, the recompense of evil...⚡ Evil’s Reward: Like Christian reaping what sown (Galatians 6:7). Wrongdoers face humiliation.
10:28وَيَوْمَ نَحْشُرُهُمْ جَمِيعًا...Jis din hum sab ko ikattha karenge...On the Day We gather them all...⚖️ Judgment Day: Like Jewish final assembly (Joel 3:2). Idols abandoned by worshippers.
10:29فَكَفَىٰ بِاللَّهِ شَهِيدًا بَيْنَنَا وَبَيْنَكُمْ...Allah hamare aur tumhare darmiyan gawah kaafi hai...Allah is sufficient as a witness between us and you...☝️ Allah’s Witness: Like Christian God’s testimony (1 John 5:9). Idols deny being worshipped.
10:30هُنَالِكَ تَبْلُو كُلُّ نَفْسٍ مَا أَسْلَفَتْ...Wahan har nafs apne kiye ka imtehaan legi...There every soul will test what it sent before...⚖️ Deeds Tested: Like Jewish accounting (Ecclesiastes 12:14). Souls face their past actions.
10:31قُلْ مَنْ يَرْزُقُكُمْ مِنَ السَّمَاءِ وَالْأَرْضِ...Kaho, “Kaun tumhein aasman aur zameen se rizq deta hai...”Say, “Who provides for you from the heavens and earth...”☝️ Allah’s Provision: Like Jewish provider God (Psalm 104:14). Challenges idol-worshippers to recognize Allah.
10:32فَذَٰلِكُمُ اللَّهُ رَبُّكُمُ الْحَقُّ...Yeh hai Allah, tumhara Rab, haq...That is Allah, your true Lord...🌟 True Lord: Like Christian one God (1 Timothy 2:5). Allah alone is the reality beyond falsehood.
10:33كَذَٰلِكَ حَقَّتْ كَلِمَةُ رَبِّكَ عَلَى الَّذِينَ فَسَقُوا...Aise hi tere Rab ka hukm fasiqon par sacch hua...Thus the word of your Lord is fulfilled against the defiantly disobedient...⚡ Divine Decree: Like Jewish judgment on rebels (Isaiah 1:28). Disobedient face confirmed punishment.
10:34قُلْ هَلْ مِنْ شُرَكَائِكُمْ مَنْ يَبْدَأُ الْخَلْقَ...Kaho, “Kya tumhare shareekon mein koi hai jo khalq shuru karta hai...”Say, “Is there among your partners one who originates creation...”🚫 Idols’ Incapacity: Like Jewish lifeless idols (Psalm 115:4). None but Allah creates or resurrects.
10:35قُلْ هَلْ مِنْ شُرَكَائِكُمْ مَنْ يَهْدِي إِلَى الْحَقِّ...Kaho, “Kya tumhare shareekon mein koi hai jo haq ki taraf hidayat deta hai...”Say, “Is there among your partners one who guides to the truth...”🌟 Allah’s Guidance: Like Christian true path (John 16:13). Only Allah leads to truth, not idols.
10:36وَمَا يَتَّبِعُ أَكْثَرُهُمْ إِلَّا ظَنًّا...Aksar log sirf gumaan ki pairvi karte hain...Most of them follow only assumption...🚫 False Assumptions: Like Jewish vain traditions (Jeremiah 10:3). Disbelievers rely on baseless beliefs.
10:37وَمَا كَانَ هَٰذَا الْقُرْآنُ أَنْ يُفْتَرَىٰ...Yeh Quran aisa nahi ke koi isse bana sake...This Quran could not have been produced by anyone other than Allah...📜 Quran’s Authenticity: Like Christian divine Word (2 Timothy 3:16). Quran confirms prior scriptures.
10:38أَمْ يَقُولُونَ افْتَرَاهُ...Kya woh kehte hain ke usne isse ghad liya...Or do they say, “He invented it...”🚫 False Accusation: Like Jewish doubting Moses (Numbers 16:3). Challenge to produce a surah like Quran.
10:39بَلْ كَذَّبُوا بِمَا لَمْ يُحِيطُوا بِعِلْمِهِ...Balki unhone usse jhutlaya jiska ilm unhein nahi...Rather, they denied what they could not comprehend...😔 Ignorant Denial: Like Christian rejecting truth (John 8:32). Disbelievers dismiss without understanding.
10:40وَمِنْهُمْ مَنْ يُؤْمِنُ بِهِ وَمِنْهُمْ مَنْ لَا يُؤْمِنُ...Un mein se kuchh is par imaan laayenge, kuchh nahi laayenge...Among them are those who believe in it, and those who do not...⚖️ Diverse Responses: Like Christian mixed reception (Acts 17:32). Some accept, others reject Quran.

Prophet separates from deniers.
10:42وَمِنْهُمْ مَنْ يَسْتَمِعُونَ إِلَيْكَ...Un mein se kuchh tujhe sunte hain...Among them are those who listen to you...😔 Deaf to Truth: Like Christian closed ears (Matthew 13:15). Some hear Quran but remain unmoved.
10:43وَمِنْهُمْ مَنْ يَنْظُرُ إِلَيْكَ...Un mein se kuchh tujhe dekhte hain...Among them are those who look at you...😔 Blind to Truth: Like Jewish blind eyes (Isaiah 6:10). Disbelievers see Prophet but lack insight.
10:44إِنَّ اللَّهَ لَا يَظْلِمُ النَّاسَ شَيْئًا...Allah logon par zara bhi zulm nahi karta...Allah does not wrong people at all...⚖️ Divine Justice: Like Christian fair judgment (Romans 2:11). Humans wrong themselves by disbelief.
10:45وَيَوْمَ يَحْشُرُهُمْ كَأَنْ لَمْ يَلْبَثُوا...Jis din woh sab ko ikattha karega, goya woh sirf ek ghanta rahe...On the Day He gathers them, as if they had stayed but an hour...⚖️ Brief Life: Like Christian fleeting time (James 4:14). Worldly life feels momentary on Judgment Day.

10:47وَلِكُلِّ أُمَّةٍ رَسُولٌ...Har ummat ke liye ek Rasool hai...For every nation is a messenger...📜 Universal Messengers: Like Christian global gospel (Matthew 24:14). Every people sent a guide.

10:49قُلْ لَا أَمْلِكُ لِنَفْسِي ضَرًّا وَلَا نَفْعًا...Kaho, “Main apne liye na nuqsaan na faida rakhta hoon...”Say, “I have no power to harm or benefit myself...”🌟 Prophet’s Limits: Like Christian human Jesus (John 5:30). Prophet depends on Allah’s will.
10:50قُلْ أَرَأَيْتُمْ إِنْ أَتَاكُمْ عَذَابُهُ...Kaho, “Batao agar uska azaab raat ya din aaye...”Say, “What if His punishment comes to you by night or day...”⚡ Sudden Punishment: Like Jewish unexpected wrath (Amos 5:18). Disbelievers warned of swift judgment.
10:51أَثُمَّ إِذَا مَا وَقَعَ آمَنْتُمْ بِهِ...Kya jab yeh waqia ho jaye tab tum imaan laaoge...Will you believe in it when it has occurred...🚫 Late Faith: Like Christian closed door (Matthew 25:10). Belief after judgment is too late.
10:52ثُمَّ قِيلَ لِلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا ذُوقُوا عَذَابَ الْخُلْدِ...Phir zalimon se kaha jayega, “Hamesha ka azaab chakh lo...”Then it will be said to the wrongdoers, “Taste eternal punishment...”🔥 Eternal Torment: Like Jewish unending fire (Isaiah 33:14). Wrongdoers face lasting consequences.
10:53وَيَسْتَنْبِئُونَكَ أَحَقٌّ هُوَ...Woh tujh se poochhte hain, “Kya yeh sach hai...”They ask you, “Is it true...”❓ Questioning Truth: Like Christian doubting resurrection (1 Corinthians 15:12). Prophet affirms reality of judgment.
10:54وَلَوْ أَنَّ لِكُلِّ نَفْسٍ ظَلَمَتْ مَا فِي الْأَرْضِ...Agar har zalim nafs ke paas zameen ki har cheez hoti...If every soul that wronged had everything on earth...😔 No Ransom: Like Jewish no escape (Psalm 49:7). Wrongdoers cannot buy redemption.
10:55أَلَا إِنَّ لِلَّهِ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ...Suno, aasman aur zameen mein jo hai sab Allah ka hai...Unquestionably, to Allah belongs whatever is in the heavens and earth...☝️ Allah’s Dominion: Like Christian all belongs to God (1 Chronicles 29:11). Everything is His.
10:56هُوَ يُحْيِي وَيُمِيتُ وَإِلَيْهِ تُرْجَعُونَ...Wahi zinda karta hai aur marta hai, aur usi ki taraf tum wapas jaoge...He gives life and causes death, and to Him you will return...🌟 Life and Death: Like Jewish God’s power (Deuteronomy 32:39). Allah controls existence and return.
10:57يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ قَدْ جَاءَتْكُمْ مَوْعِظَةٌ...Aye logon, tumhare paas ek naseehat aayi hai...O mankind, there has come to you an admonition...📜 Quran’s Healing: Like Christian healing Word (Psalm 107:20). Quran is guidance and mercy for hearts.
10:58قُلْ بِفَضْلِ اللَّهِ وَبِرَحْمَتِهِ...Kaho, “Allah ke fazl aur rehmat se...”Say, “In the bounty and mercy of Allah...”🌟 Rejoice in Mercy: Like Jewish joy in God’s grace (Psalm 30:5). Believers celebrate divine favor.
10:59قُلْ أَرَأَيْتُمْ مَا أَنْزَلَ اللَّهُ لَكُمْ مِنْ رِزْقٍ...Kaho, “Batao jo rizq Allah ne tumhein diya...”Say, “Have you seen what Allah has sent down for you of provision...”⚖️ Questioning Laws: Like Jewish food laws (Leviticus 11:2). Challenges disbelievers’ arbitrary prohibitions.
10:60وَمَا ظَنُّ الَّذِينَ يَفْتَرُونَ عَلَى اللَّهِ الْكَذِبَ...Jo Allah par jhoot ghadte hain, unka kya guman hai...What is the assumption of those who invent lies against Allah...⚡ Liars’ Fate: Like Christian false teachers’ judgment (2 Peter 2:1). Liars face reckoning on Judgment Day.
10:61وَمَا تَكُونُ فِي شَأْنٍ وَمَا تَتْلُو مِنْهُ مِنْ قُرْآنٍ...Tu kisi kaam mein ho ya Quran padhta ho...In whatever matter you are, or whatever you recite of the Quran...🌟 Allah’s Oversight: Like Christian all-seeing God (Proverbs 15:3). No action escapes Allah’s knowledge.
10:62أَلَا إِنَّ أَوْلِيَاءَ اللَّهِ لَا خَوْفٌ عَلَيْهِمْ...Suno, Allah ke waliyon par na dar hai na gham...Unquestionably, the allies of Allah have no fear nor grief...🌴 Saints’ Security: Like Jewish protected righteous (Psalm 23:4). Allah’s friends are safe and joyful.
10:63الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَكَانُوا يَتَّقُونَ...Jo imaan laaye aur taqwa rakhte the...Those who believe and were fearing Allah...🌟 Faith and Piety: Like Christian blessed faithful (Matthew 5:8). Belief and God-consciousness define Allah’s allies.
10:64لَهُمُ الْبُشْرَىٰ فِي الْحَيَاةِ الدُّنْيَا...Unke liye dunya aur aakhirat mein khushkhabri hai...For them are good tidings in the worldly life and the Hereafter...🕊️ Good Tidings: Like Christian gospel joy (Luke 2:10). Believers receive divine assurances.
10:65وَلَا يَحْزُنْكَ قَوْلُهُمْ...Unki baaton se tu ghamgeen na ho...Let not their words grieve you...🌟 Prophet’s Comfort: Like Jewish prophet’s solace (Isaiah 51:7). Allah’s might supports the Prophet.
10:66أَلَا إِنَّ لِلَّهِ مَنْ فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَنْ فِي الْأَرْضِ...Suno, aasman aur zameen mein jo hai sab Allah ka hai...Unquestionably, to Allah belongs whoever is in the heavens and earth...☝️ Universal Dominion: Like Christian all creation’s Lord (Colossians 1:16). Idols have no followers.
10:67هُوَ الَّذِي جَعَلَ لَكُمُ اللَّيْلَ لِتَسْكُنُوا...Wahi hai jisne raat ko sukoon ke liye banaya...It is He Who made the night for you to rest...🌙 Night and Day: Like Jewish creation’s purpose (Genesis 1:14). Night for rest, day for sight are divine signs.
10:68قَالُوا اتَّخَذَ اللَّهُ وَلَدًا...Unhone kaha, “Allah ne beta ikhtiyar kiya...”They say, “Allah has taken a son...”🚫 False Sonship: Like Christian clarifying Jesus (John 1:18). Rejects claim of Allah having a child.
10:69قُلْ إِنَّ الَّذِينَ يَفْتَرُونَ عَلَى اللَّهِ الْكَذِبَ...Kaho, “Jo Allah par jhoot ghadte hain, woh kaamyaab nahi hote...”Say, “Those who invent lies against Allah will not succeed...”🚫 Liars Fail: Like Jewish false prophets’ doom (Jeremiah 23:32). False claims about Allah lead to failure.
10:70مَتَاعٌ فِي الدُّنْيَا ثُمَّ إِلَيْنَا مَرْجِعُهُمْ...Dunya mein thodi si zindagi, phir hamari taraf wapsi...A brief enjoyment in this world, then to Us is their return...⚡ Temporary Gain: Like Christian fleeting pleasures (Hebrews 11:25). Liars face severe punishment after brief life.
10:71وَاتْلُ عَلَيْهِمْ نَبَأَ نُوحٍ...Unhein Nuh ka qissa suna...Recite to them the story of Noah...📜 Noah’s Mission: Like Christian Noah’s ark (1 Peter 3:20). Noah warned his people, faced rejection.
10:72فَإِنْ تَوَلَّيْتُمْ فَمَا سَأَلْتُكُمْ مِنْ أَجْرٍ...Agar tum munh pher lo, toh maine tum se koi ujrat nahi maangi...If you turn away, I have not asked you for any reward...🌟 Prophet’s Selflessness: Like Jewish unpaid prophets (Micah 3:11). Noah sought no reward, only obedience.
10:73فَكَذَّبُوهُ فَنَجَّيْنَاهُ...Unhone usse jhutlaya, toh humne usse aur uske saathiyon ko bacha liya...They denied him, so We saved him and those with him...🛶 Noah’s Salvation: Like Jewish flood deliverance (Genesis 7:23). Noah and believers saved, deniers drowned.
10:74ثُمَّ بَعَثْنَا مِنْ بَعْدِهِ رُسُلًا...Phir humne uske baad doosre rusul bheje...Then We sent after him messengers...📜 Successive Prophets: Like Christian ongoing revelation (Hebrews 1:1). Nations rejected later messengers.
10:75ثُمَّ بَعَثْنَا مِنْ بَعْدِهِمْ مُوسَىٰ وَهَارُونَ...Phir humne unke baad Musa aur Haroon ko bheja...Then We sent after them Moses and Aaron...📜 Moses’ Mission: Like Jewish exodus leader (Exodus 3:10). Moses and Aaron sent to Pharaoh with signs.
10:76فَلَمَّا جَاءَهُمُ الْحَقُّ مِنْ عِنْدِنَا...Jab hamari taraf se haq unke paas aaya...When the truth came to them from Us...🚫 Pharaoh’s Denial: Like Christian hardened hearts (Romans 2:5). Pharaoh’s people rejected clear signs.
10:77قَالَ مُوسَىٰ أَتَقُولُونَ لِلْحَقِّ لَمَّا جَاءَكُمْ...Musa ne kaha, “Kya tum haq ke bare mein kehte ho jab yeh tumhare paas aaya...”Moses said, “Do you say of the truth when it has come to you...”❓ Challenging Denial: Like Jewish prophetic rebuke (Amos 5:10). Moses questions rejection of truth.
10:78قَالُوا أَجِئْتَنَا لِتَلْفِتَنَا...Unhone kaha, “Kya tu hamein hamare baap-dada ke tareeqe se hataane aaya...”They said, “Have you come to turn us away from our fathers’ ways...”🚫 Clinging to Tradition: Like Christian ancestral error (Galatians 1:14). Pharaoh’s people defend false customs.
10:79وَقَالَ فِرْعَوْنُ ائْتُونِي بِكُلِّ سَاحِرٍ عَلِيمٍ...Fir’aun ne kaha, “Har mahir jadugar ko mere paas lao...”Pharaoh said, “Bring me every learned magician...”😈 Pharaoh’s Defiance: Like Jewish false priests (2 Chronicles 13:9). Pharaoh relies on magicians to counter truth.
10:80فَلَمَّا جَاءَ السَّحَرَةُ قَالَ لَهُمْ مُوسَىٰ...Jab jadugar aaye, Musa ne un se kaha...When the magicians came, Moses said to them...⚔️ Moses’ Challenge: Like Christian truth vs. falsehood (Acts 13:8). Moses calls magicians to show their power.
10:81فَلَمَّا أَلْقَوْا قَالَ مُوسَىٰ مَا جِئْتُمْ بِهِ السِّحْرُ...Jab unhone dala, Musa ne kaha, “Jo tum laaye ho woh jadoo hai...”When they threw, Moses said, “What you have brought is magic...”🌟 Exposing Magic: Like Jewish divine power (Exodus 7:12). Moses declares magic inferior to Allah’s truth.
10:82وَيُحِقُّ اللَّهُ الْحَقَّ بِكَلِمَاتِهِ...Allah apne kalimaat se haq ko saabit karta hai...Allah establishes the truth by His words...🌟 Truth Prevails: Like Christian victory of truth (John 8:32). Allah upholds truth despite falsehood.
10:83فَمَا آمَنَ لِمُوسَىٰ إِلَّا ذُرِّيَّةٌ مِنْ قَوْمِهِ...Musa par sirf uski qoum ke kuchh naujawanon ne imaan laya...None believed in Moses except some youths of his people...😔 Few Believers: Like Christian small flock (Luke 12:32). Fear of Pharaoh limited faith to a few.
10:84وَقَالَ مُوسَىٰ يَا قَوْمِ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ آمَنْتُمْ...Musa ne kaha, “Aye meri qoum, agar tum Allah par imaan laaye ho...”Moses said, “O my people, if you have believed in Allah...”🙏 Trust in Allah: Like Jewish call to faith (Exodus 14:13). Moses urges reliance on Allah.
10:85فَقَالُوا عَلَى اللَّهِ تَوَكَّلْنَا...Unhone kaha, “Humne Allah par bharosa kiya...”They said, “Upon Allah we rely...”🌟 Faithful Reliance: Like Christian trust in God (Proverbs 3:5). Believers affirm dependence on Allah.
10:86وَنَجِّنَا بِرَحْمَتِكَ مِنَ الْقَوْمِ الْكَافِرِينَ...Hamein apni rehmat se kafir qoum se bacha...Deliver us by Your mercy from the disbelieving people...🙏 Plea for Salvation: Like Jewish cry for rescue (Psalm 79:9). Believers seek divine deliverance.
10:87وَأَوْحَيْنَا إِلَىٰ مُوسَىٰ وَأَخِيهِ أَنْ تَبَوَّآ...Humne Musa aur uske bhai ko wahi ki ke apni qoum ke liye ghar banayein...We revealed to Moses and his brother to appoint houses for your people...🏠 Preparation in Egypt: Like Jewish organizing for exodus (Exodus 12:3). Moses and Aaron prepare for deliverance.
10:88وَقَالَ مُوسَىٰ رَبَّنَا إِنَّكَ آتَيْتَ فِرْعَوْنَ...Musa ne kaha, “Aye hamare Rab, tune Fir’aun ko zeenat di...”Moses said, “Our Lord, You have given Pharaoh adornment...”🙏 Prayer Against Oppressors: Like Jewish plea against enemies (Psalm 83:9). Moses prays to remove Pharaoh’s power.
10:89قَالَ قَدْ أُجِيبَتْ دَعْوَتُكُمَا...Usne kaha, “Tum dono ki dua qubool ki gayi...”He said, “Your prayer has been answered...”🕊️ Answered Prayer: Like Christian granted requests (John 15:7). Allah accepts Moses and Aaron’s supplication.
10:90وَجَاوَزْنَا بِبَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ الْبَحْرَ...Humne Bani Israeel ko samandar paar karaya...We took the Children of Israel across the sea...🌊 Sea Crossing: Like Jewish Red Sea miracle (Exodus 14:22). Pharaoh’s late faith rejected as he drowns.
10:91آلْآنَ وَقَدْ عَصَيْتَ قَبْلُ...Ab, jab tune pehle naframaani ki...Now, when you have disobeyed before...🚫 Too Late: Like Christian untimely faith (Luke 13:25). Pharaoh’s belief at death is futile.
10:92فَالْيَوْمَ نُنَجِّيكَ بِبَدَنِكَ...Aaj hum tujhe tere jism ke saath bacha lenge...Today We will save you in your body...⚖️ Pharaoh’s Body: Like Jewish sign of judgment (Deuteronomy 28:68). Pharaoh’s body preserved as a sign.
10:93وَلَقَدْ بَوَّأْنَا بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ مُبَوَّأَ صِدْقٍ...Humne Bani Israeel ko sachchi jagah basaya...We settled the Children of Israel in a true settlement...🏞️ Israel’s Settlement: Like Jewish promised land (Joshua 21:43). Allah provided for them, yet they differed.
10:94فَإِنْ كُنْتَ فِي شَكٍّ مِمَّا أَنْزَلْنَا...Agar tu usmein shak mein hai jo humne utara...If you are in doubt about what We have revealed...📜 Consult Scriptures: Like Christian confirming truth (Acts 17:11). Prophet urged to ask People of the Book if in doubt.
10:95وَلَا تَكُونَنَّ مِنَ الَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا بِآيَاتِ اللَّهِ...Aur un mein se na hona jinhone Allah ki aayaton ko jhutlaya...Do not be among those who deny Allah’s signs...🚫 Avoid Denial: Like Jewish rejecting signs (Numbers 14:11). Denial leads to loss.
10:96إِنَّ الَّذِينَ حَقَّتْ عَلَيْهِمْ كَلِمَةُ رَبِّكَ...Jin par tere Rab ka hukm sacch hua...Those upon whom your Lord’s word has come true...⚡ Sealed Fate: Like Christian hardened hearts (Romans 11:8). Some destined to reject faith.
10:97وَلَوْ جَاءَتْهُمْ كُلُّ آيَةٍ حَتَّىٰ يَرَوُا الْعَذَابَ...Chahe har nishani unke paas aaye, jab tak woh azaab na dekhein...Even if every sign came to them, until they see the painful punishment...😔 Stubborn Denial: Like Jewish unyielding hearts (Exodus 7:14). Disbelievers reject all signs until punishment.
10:98فَلَوْلَا كَانَتْ قَرْيَةٌ آمَنَتْ فَنَفَعَهَا إِيمَانُهَا...Koi basti aisi kyun na thi jo imaan laayi aur uska imaan uske kaam aaya...Why was there no city that believed and benefited from its faith...🕊️ Jonah’s People: Like Christian Nineveh’s repentance (Jonah 3:10). Only Jonah’s people repented, were spared.
10:99وَلَوْ شَاءَ رَبُّكَ لَآمَنَ مَنْ فِي الْأَرْضِ...Agar tera Rab chahta toh zameen ke sab log imaan laate...If your Lord had willed, everyone on earth would have believed...🌟 Free Will: Like Christian human choice (John 3:18). Allah allows choice, not forcing faith.
10:100وَمَا كَانَ لِنَفْسٍ أَنْ تُؤْمِنَ إِلَّا بِإِذْنِ اللَّهِ...Koi nafs imaan nahi la sakta siwaye Allah ke ijazat ke...No soul can believe except by Allah’s permission...🌟 Divine Permission: Like Jewish chosen faith (Deuteronomy 7:6). Belief requires Allah’s guidance.
10:101قُلِ انْظُرُوا مَاذَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ...Kaho, “Dekho aasman aur zameen mein kya hai...”Say, “Observe what is in the heavens and earth...”🌍 Reflect on Signs: Like Christian creation’s witness (Romans 1:20). Signs in nature point to Allah.
10:102فَهَلْ يَنْتَظِرُونَ إِلَّا مِثْلَ أَيَّامِ الَّذِينَ خَلَوْا...Kya woh intezar karte hain usi tarah ke dinon ka jo pehle guzar gaye...Do they await except the like of the days of those who passed before...⚡ Past Nations’ Fate: Like Jewish historical warning (Psalm 95:8). Disbelievers risk same destruction.
10:103ثُمَّ نُنَجِّي رُسُلَنَا وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا...Phir hum apne rusul aur imaan walon ko bacha lete hain...Then We save Our messengers and those who believe...🛡️ Divine Rescue: Like Christian salvation of faithful (1 Thessalonians 5:9). Allah saves prophets and believers.
10:104قُلْ يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ فِي شَكٍّ...Kaho, “Aye logon, agar tum mere deen mein shak mein ho...”Say, “O people, if you are in doubt about my religion...”☝️ Prophet’s Declaration: Like Jewish clear call (Isaiah 48:16). Prophet affirms monotheism, rejects idols.
10:105وَأَنْ أَقِمْ وَجْهَكَ لِلدِّينِ حَنِيفًا...Aur yeh ke apna chehra deen ke liye hanif rakh...And to direct your face toward the religion, as a monotheist...🌟 Pure Monotheism: Like Christian one God (1 Corinthians 8:6). Prophet commanded to uphold pure faith.
10:106وَلَا تَدْعُ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ مَا لَا يَنْفَعُكَ...Allah ke siwa usse na pukaar jo na faida de na nuqsaan...Do not call upon besides Allah what neither benefits nor harms...🚫 Avoid Idolatry: Like Jewish shunning idols (Exodus 20:4). Worship only Allah, not powerless beings.
10:107وَإِنْ يَمْسَسْكَ اللَّهُ بِضُرٍّ فَلَا كَاشِفَ لَهُ...Agar Allah tujhe takleef de, toh koi usse hataane wala nahi...If Allah touches you with harm, none can remove it but He...🌟 Allah’s Control: Like Christian God’s sovereignty (Job 1:21). Only Allah relieves or sends affliction.
10:108قُلْ يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ قَدْ جَاءَكُمُ الْحَقُّ...Kaho, “Aye logon, tumhare paas haq aa chuka hai...”Say, “O people, the truth has come to you...”📜 Truth Proclaimed: Like Christian gospel call (Mark 16:15). Accepting truth leads to guidance; rejecting to loss.
10:109وَاتَّبِعْ مَا يُوحَىٰ إِلَيْكَ وَاصْبِرْ...Jo tujh par wahi ki jati hai uski pairvi kar aur sabr kar...Follow what is revealed to you and be patient...🌟 Prophet’s Duty: Like Jewish steadfast prophets (Jeremiah 1:7). Prophet urged to follow revelation and endure.
