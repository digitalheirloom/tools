Surah Al-Anfal

(Chapter 8: The Spoils of War) - Verses 1-75 Analysis 📖

📜 Introduction: Setting the Scene

  • Place and Context of Revelation

    • Historical Setting: Revealed in Medina (circa 623-624 CE), primarily after the Battle of Badr (2 AH/624 CE), the first major victory for Muslims against the Quraysh. Some verses address later events, like the Treaty of Hudaybiyyah.
    • Geographical Context: Medina, a growing Muslim community hub, where the Prophet Muhammad established a nascent Islamic state amidst internal and external challenges.
    • Circumstances: Named “The Spoils of War” (Al-Anfal), the surah addresses the distribution of war spoils post-Badr, the importance of unity, obedience to God and His Messenger, and lessons from the battle. It emphasizes faith, discipline, and trust in divine support during conflict.

  • 🔑 Key Themes and Topics

    • ☝️ Tawhid and Obedience: Reinforces submission to God and the Prophet’s leadership.
    • ⚔️ Jihad and Unity: Guides Muslims on warfare ethics, preparation, and communal solidarity.
    • ⚖️ Justice in Spoils: Outlines fair distribution of war gains, prioritizing divine law over personal gain.
    • 🌟 Divine Support: Highlights God’s aid in battles, encouraging reliance on Him.

  • 🌍 Relevance Today

    • ⏳ Ethical Warfare: Offers principles for just conflict, relevant to modern discussions on war ethics.
    • 🤝 Community Cohesion: Emphasizes unity and discipline, applicable to organizational and social contexts.
    • 💡 Faith in Adversity: Encourages trust in divine wisdom during challenges, resonating across faiths.

8:1يَسْأَلُونَكَ عَنِ الْأَنْفَالِ...Woh tujh se anfaal ke bare mein poochhte hain...They ask you about the spoils of war...⚖️ Spoils Distribution: Like Jewish division of spoils (Numbers 31:27). Spoils belong to Allah and His Messenger for just allocation.
8:2إِنَّمَا الْمُؤْمِنُونَ الَّذِينَ إِذَا ذُكِرَ اللَّهُ...Sachche momin woh hain jo jab Allah ka zikr hota hai...True believers are those whose hearts tremble when Allah is mentioned...🌟 True Faith: Like Christian fear of God (Acts 9:31). Believers feel awe and strengthen resolve.
8:3الَّذِينَ يُقِيمُونَ الصَّلَاةَ وَمِمَّا رَزَقْنَاهُمْ...Jo namaaz qaim karte hain aur jo humne unhein diya usmein se kharch karte hain...Who establish prayer and spend from what We have provided...🙏 Worship and Charity: Like Jewish tzedakah (Deuteronomy 15:11). Marks of faith include prayer and giving.
8:4أُولَٰئِكَ هُمُ الْمُؤْمِنُونَ حَقًّا...Wahi sachche momin hain...Those are the true believers...🌴 Rewards of Faith: Like Christian eternal reward (Matthew 5:12). Believers gain high ranks and forgiveness.
8:5كَمَا أَخْرَجَكَ رَبُّكَ مِنْ بَيْتِكَ بِالْحَقِّ...Jaise tere Rab ne tujhe tere ghar se haq ke saath nikala...As your Lord brought you out of your home in truth...🧑 Divine Command: Like Jewish exodus (Exodus 12:51). Some believers hesitated but followed the Prophet.
8:6يُجَادِلُونَكَ فِي الْحَقِّ بَعْدَمَا تَبَيَّنَ...Woh tujh se haq ke bare mein jhagdte hain baad iske ke woh zaahir ho chuka...They disputed with you about the truth after it was clear...🚫 Doubt Despite Clarity: Like Christian doubting disciples (John 20:25). Warns against arguing after truth is evident.
8:7وَإِذْ يَعِدُكُمُ اللَّهُ إِحْدَى الطَّائِفَتَيْنِ...Jab Allah tumhein do girohon mein se ek ka waada de raha tha...When Allah promised you one of the two groups...⚔️ Divine Promise: Like Jewish victory assurance (Deuteronomy 20:4). God promised victory over the enemy or caravan.
8:8لِيُحِقَّ الْحَقَّ وَيُبْطِلَ الْبَاطِلَ...Taki haq ko saabit kare aur baatil ko mita de...To establish the truth and abolish falsehood...🌟 Truth Prevails: Like Christian triumph of good (Romans 12:21). God’s aim is to uphold truth.
8:9إِذْ تَسْتَغِيثُونَ رَبَّكُمْ فَاسْتَجَابَ لَكُمْ...Jab tum apne Rab se fariyaad kar rahe the, toh usne tumhari sun li...When you sought help from your Lord, and He answered...🙏 Divine Aid: Like Jewish cry for help (Psalm 107:6). God sent angels to support at Badr.
8:10وَمَا جَعَلَهُ اللَّهُ إِلَّا بُشْرَىٰ...Allah ne isse sirf khushkhabri banaya...Allah made it only good news...🕊️ Encouragement: Like Christian comfort in trials (2 Corinthians 1:4). Angels were a sign to strengthen resolve.
8:11إِذْ يُغَشِّيكُمُ النُّعَاسَ أَمَنَةً مِنْهُ...Jab woh tum par susti daal raha tha jo uski taraf se aman thi...When He covered you with drowsiness as security from Him...😴 Divine Calm: Like Jewish peace in battle (Psalm 4:8). God sent rain and tranquility at Badr.
8:12إِذْ يُوحِي رَبُّكَ إِلَى الْمَلَائِكَةِ...Jab tera Rab farishton ko wahi bhej raha tha...When your Lord inspired the angels...😇 Angelic Support: Like Jewish angelic aid (2 Kings 6:17). Angels struck fear into enemies’ hearts.
8:13ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّهُمْ شَاقُّوا اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ...Yeh is liye ke unhone Allah aur uske Rasool ka mukhalifa kiya...That is because they opposed Allah and His Messenger...⚡ Punishment for Opposition: Like Christian judgment for rebellion (Romans 13:2). Defiance brings destruction.
8:14ذَٰلِكُمْ فَذُوقُوهُ وَأَنَّ لِلْكَافِرِينَ عَذَابَ النَّارِ...Yeh hai, isse chakh lo, aur kafiron ke liye aag ka azaab hai...That is for you, so taste it; for the disbelievers is the punishment of the Fire...🔥 Taste of Punishment: Like Jewish warning of wrath (Deuteronomy 32:22). Disbelievers face consequences.
8:15يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِذَا لَقِيتُمُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا...Aye imaan walon, jab tum kafiron se mulaqat karo...O you who believe, when you meet those who disbelieve...⚔️ Stand Firm: Like Christian courage in trials (Ephesians 6:13). Believers urged not to flee in battle.
8:16وَمَنْ يُوَلِّهِمْ يَوْمَئِذٍ دُبُرَهُ...Jo us din un se peeth pher lega...Whoever turns his back to them on that day...🚫 Fleeing Forbidden: Like Jewish call to bravery (Deuteronomy 20:3). Fleeing incurs divine wrath unless strategic.
8:17فَلَمْ تَقْتُلُوهُمْ وَلَٰكِنَّ اللَّهَ قَتَلَهُمْ...Tumne unhein nahi maara, balki Allah ne unhein maara...You did not kill them, but Allah killed them...🌟 God’s Victory: Like Jewish divine deliverance (1 Samuel 17:47). Victory attributed to God, not human effort.
8:18ذَٰلِكُمْ وَأَنَّ اللَّهَ مُوهِنُ كَيْدِ الْكَافِرِينَ...Yeh hai, aur Allah kafiron ke makr ko kamzor karta hai...That is so, and Allah weakens the plan of the disbelievers...⚖️ Foil Enemies’ Plans: Like Christian God’s sovereignty (Psalm 33:10). God thwarts disbelievers’ schemes.
8:19إِنْ تَسْتَفْتِحُوا فَقَدْ جَاءَكُمُ الْفَتْحُ...Agar tum faisla maangte ho, toh faisla aa chuka hai...If you sought a decision, the decision has come to you...⚡ Judgment Delivered: Like Jewish divine verdict (Isaiah 10:23). Disbelievers’ defeat at Badr was God’s answer.
8:20يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا أَطِيعُوا اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ...Aye imaan walon, Allah aur uske Rasool ki ita’at karo...O you who believe, obey Allah and His Messenger...🧑 Obedience Urged: Like Christian submission to authority (Hebrews 13:17). Believers must follow divine orders.
8:21وَلَا تَكُونُوا كَالَّذِينَ قَالُوا سَمِعْنَا وَهُمْ لَا يَسْمَعُونَ...Aur un jaise na bano jo kehte hain “Humne suna” jabki woh sunte nahi...Do not be like those who say, “We hear,” but do not hear...🚫 Avoid Hypocrisy: Like Christian empty words (Matthew 7:21). Warns against pretending to obey.
8:22إِنَّ شَرَّ الدَّوَابِّ عِنْدَ اللَّهِ الصُّمُّ الْبُكْمُ...Allah ke nazdeek sab se bura jaanwar woh hai jo goonga aur behra hai...The worst of creatures before Allah are the deaf and dumb...😔 Spiritual Blindness: Like Jewish stubborn hearts (Isaiah 6:10). Those who ignore truth are lowest.
8:23وَلَوْ عَلِمَ اللَّهُ فِيهِمْ خَيْرًا لَأَسْمَعَهُمْ...Agar Allah un mein koi khair dekhta toh unhein sunata...Had Allah known any good in them, He would have made them hear...🚫 Divine Rejection: Like Christian closed ears (Matthew 13:15). God denies guidance to the unrepentant.
8:24يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا اسْتَجِيبُوا لِلَّهِ...Aye imaan walon, Allah aur uske Rasool ke bulane par labbaik kaho...O you who believe, respond to Allah and His Messenger...🙏 Answer the Call: Like Jewish covenant response (Exodus 24:7). Believers urged to heed divine summons.
8:25وَاتَّقُوا فِتْنَةً لَا تُصِيبَنَّ الَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا...Aur us fitne se daro jo sirf zalimon tak mehdood nahi rahega...Fear a trial that will not only strike the wrongdoers...⚠️ Communal Trial: Like Christian shared consequences (1 Corinthians 5:6). Sin affects the whole community.
8:26وَاذْكُرُوا إِذْ أَنْتُمْ قَلِيلٌ مُسْتَضْعَفُونَ...Yaad karo jab tum thode aur kamzor the...Remember when you were few and oppressed...🛡️ Divine Favor: Like Jewish deliverance (Psalm 136:23). God strengthened Muslims in weakness.
8:27يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَخُونُوا اللَّهَ...Aye imaan walon, Allah aur Rasool ke saath khayanat na karo...O you who believe, do not betray Allah and the Messenger...🚫 Avoid Betrayal: Like Christian fidelity (Luke 16:10). Warns against misusing trusts or spoils.
8:28وَاعْلَمُوا أَنَّمَا أَمْوَالُكُمْ وَأَوْلَادُكُمْ فِتْنَةٌ...Jano ke tumhara maal aur aulaad fitna hai...Know that your wealth and children are a trial...⚖️ Test of Priorities: Like Hindu detachment (Bhagavad Gita 2:47). Wealth and family test faith.
8:29يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِنْ تَتَّقُوا اللَّهَ...Aye imaan walon, agar tum Allah se daro...O you who believe, if you fear Allah...🌟 Divine Discernment: Like Christian wisdom (James 1:5). God grants insight to distinguish right from wrong.
8:30وَإِذْ يَمْكُرُ بِكَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا...Jab kafir tere khilaaf makr kar rahe the...When the disbelievers plotted against you...😈 Foiled Plots: Like Jewish protection of Esther (Esther 7:10). God thwarted Quraysh’s schemes in Mecca.
8:31وَإِذَا تُتْلَىٰ عَلَيْهِمْ آيَاتُنَا...Jab hamari aayatein un par padhi jati hain...When Our verses are recited to them...🚫 Mocking Scripture: Like Christian scorners (2 Peter 3:3). Disbelievers dismiss Quran as old tales.
8:32وَإِذْ قَالُوا اللَّهُمَّ إِنْ كَانَ هَٰذَا هُوَ الْحَقَّ...Jab unhone kaha, “Aye Allah, agar yeh haq hai...”When they said, “O Allah, if this is the truth...”❓ Insincere Prayer: Like Jewish false vows (Malachi 1:14). Disbelievers mockingly demand a sign.
8:33وَمَا كَانَ اللَّهُ لِيُعَذِّبَهُمْ وَأَنْتَ فِيهِمْ...Allah unhein saza nahi deta jab tu un mein hai...Allah would not punish them while you are among them...🛡️ Prophet’s Presence: Like Christian intercessor (1 Timothy 2:5). Prophet’s presence delays punishment.
8:34وَمَا لَهُمْ أَلَّا يُعَذِّبَهُمُ اللَّهُ...Unhein kyun na saza de Allah jab woh logon ko masjid se rokte hain...Why should Allah not punish them when they prevent access to the Sacred Mosque...🚫 Blocking Worship: Like Jewish temple desecration (Ezekiel 8:6). Quraysh’s control of Ka’bah condemned.
8:35وَمَا كَانَ صَلَاتُهُمْ عِنْدَ الْبَيْتِ إِلَّا مُكَاءً...Unki namaaz Ka’bah ke paas sirf seeti aur tali thi...Their prayer at the House was only whistling and clapping...🚫 False Worship: Like Christian empty rituals (Matthew 15:8). Quraysh’s practices mocked as meaningless.
8:36إِنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا يُنْفِقُونَ أَمْوَالَهُمْ...Jo kafir hain, woh apna maal kharch karte hain...The disbelievers spend their wealth to hinder Allah’s way...😈 Misguided Spending: Like Jewish wasted offerings (Malachi 1:10). Quraysh’s wealth used against Islam.
8:37لِيَمِيزَ اللَّهُ الْخَبِيثَ مِنَ الطَّيِّبِ...Taki Allah gande ko paak se alag kare...So Allah may separate the impure from the pure...⚖️ Divine Sorting: Like Christian wheat and tares (Matthew 13:30). God distinguishes believers from disbelievers.
8:38قُلْ لِلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا إِنْ يَنْتَهُوا يُغْفَرْ لَهُمْ...Kafiron se kaho, agar woh baaz aa jayein toh unhein maaf kiya jayega...Tell the disbelievers, if they desist, their past will be forgiven...🕊️ Offer of Forgiveness: Like Christian call to repent (Acts 3:19). Disbelievers can be forgiven if they stop.
8:39وَقَاتِلُوهُمْ حَتَّىٰ لَا تَكُونَ فِتْنَةٌ...Un se lado yahan tak ke fitna khatm ho jaye...Fight them until there is no persecution...⚔️ End Persecution: Like Jewish fight for freedom (1 Maccabees 2:44). Aim is to secure worship without oppression.
8:40وَإِنْ تَوَلَّوْا فَاعْلَمُوا أَنَّ اللَّهَ مَوْلَاكُمْ...Agar woh munh pher lein, toh jano ke Allah tumhara maula hai...If they turn away, know that Allah is your Protector...🛡️ God’s Protection: Like Christian refuge (Psalm 46:1). Allah is the ultimate ally.
8:41وَاعْلَمُوا أَنَّمَا غَنِمْتُمْ مِنْ شَيْءٍ...Jano ke jo kuchh tumne ghanimat mein liya...Know that whatever you gain as spoils...⚖️ Spoils Allocation: Like Jewish tithe system (Deuteronomy 14:28). One-fifth for God’s cause, rest distributed.
8:42إِذْ أَنْتُمْ بِالْعُدْوَةِ الدُّنْيَا...Jab tum zameen ke kinaare par the...When you were on the near side of the valley...⚔️ Badr’s Setup: Like Jewish strategic battles (Joshua 8:4). Describes Badr’s terrain and divine plan.
8:43إِذْ يُرِيكَهُمُ اللَّهُ فِي مَنَامِكَ قَلِيلًا...Jab Allah ne tujhe khwab mein unhein thoda dikhaya...When Allah showed them to you in your dream as few...🌙 Divine Vision: Like Jewish prophetic dreams (Genesis 41:1). God reassured the Prophet with a vision.
8:44وَإِذْ يُرِيكُمُوهُمْ إِذِ الْتَقَيْتُمْ قَلِيلًا...Jab tum mulaqat hue, Allah ne unhein thoda dikhaya...When you met, He showed them to you as few...🌟 Psychological Aid: Like Christian courage (Philippians 4:13). God altered perceptions to boost morale.
8:45يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِذَا لَقِيتُمْ فِئَةً...Aye imaan walon, jab tum kisi giroh se mulaqat karo...O you who believe, when you meet a force...⚔️ Battle Discipline: Like Jewish war preparation (Deuteronomy 20:2). Stand firm and remember God.
8:46وَأَطِيعُوا اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ وَلَا تَنَازَعُوا...Allah aur uske Rasool ki ita’at karo aur aapas mein jhagda na karo...Obey Allah and His Messenger, and do not dispute...🤝 Unity in Battle: Like Christian harmony (1 Corinthians 1:10). Unity and obedience ensure success.
8:47وَلَا تَكُونُوا كَالَّذِينَ خَرَجُوا مِنْ دِيَارِهِمْ...Un jaise na bano jo apne ghar se ghamand mein nikle...Do not be like those who left their homes in arrogance...🚫 Avoid Arrogance: Like Jewish warning against pride (Proverbs 16:18). Quraysh’s hubris led to defeat.
8:48وَإِذْ زَيَّنَ لَهُمُ الشَّيْطَانُ أَعْمَالَهُمْ...Jab shaitaan ne unke kaam unhein sundar dikhaye...When Satan made their deeds seem fair to them...😈 Satan’s Deception: Like Christian temptation (1 Peter 5:8). Satan misled Quraysh to overconfidence.
8:49إِذْ يَقُولُ الْمُنَافِقُونَ وَالَّذِينَ فِي قُلُوبِهِمْ مَرَضٌ...Jab munafiq aur dil ke mareez kehte the...When the hypocrites and those with diseased hearts said...😔 Hypocrites’ Doubt: Like Christian false brethren (Galatians 2:4). Hypocrites mocked believers’ faith.
8:50وَلَوْ تَرَىٰ إِذْ يَتَوَفَّى الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا...Agar tu dekhe jab kafiron ki rooh nikaali jati hai...If you could see when the angels take the souls of the disbelievers...😇 Angels’ Judgment: Like Jewish angel of death (2 Kings 19:35). Angels strike disbelievers at death.
8:51ذَٰلِكَ بِمَا قَدَّمَتْ أَيْدِيكُمْ...Yeh uske wajah se jo tumhare haathon ne kiya...This is for what your hands have put forth...⚖️ Consequences of Deeds: Like Hindu karma (Bhagavad Gita 4:17). Disbelievers face results of actions.
8:52كَدَأْبِ قَوْمِ فِرْعَوْنَ...Jaise Fir’aun ki qoum ka tareeqa tha...Like the way of Pharaoh’s people...😔 Historical Parallel: Like Jewish Pharaoh’s fate (Exodus 14:28). Quraysh likened to past rejectors.
8:53ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّ اللَّهَ لَمْ يَكُ مُغَيِّرًا نِعْمَةً...Yeh is liye ke Allah apni ne’mat ko tabdeel nahi karta...Because Allah would not change a favor...🌟 Divine Consistency: Like Christian unchanging God (James 1:17). God withdraws blessings only if people change.
8:54كَدَأْبِ قَوْمِ فِرْعَوْنَ...Jaise Fir’aun ki qoum ka tareeqa tha...Like the way of Pharaoh’s people...⚡ Repeated Warning: Like Jewish lessons from history (Psalm 78:56). Past nations’ destruction as a lesson.
8:55إِنَّ شَرَّ الدَّوَابِّ عِنْدَ اللَّهِ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا...Allah ke nazdeek sab se bura jaanwar woh hai jo kafir hain...The worst of creatures before Allah are those who disbelieve...😔 Lowest Status: Like Christian rejection of truth (John 3:36). Disbelievers ranked lowest for defiance.
8:56الَّذِينَ عَاهَدْتَ مِنْهُمْ ثُمَّ يَنْقُضُونَ عَهْدَهُمْ...Jo logon se tune ahd kiya, phir woh apna ahd todte hain...Those with whom you made a treaty, then they break it...🚫 Breaking Treaties: Like Jewish covenant breakers (Jeremiah 11:10). Warns against treaty violations.
8:57فَإِمَّا تَثْقَفَنَّهُمْ فِي الْحَرْبِ...Agar tu unhein jang mein pakad le...If you encounter them in battle...⚔️ Deterrence: Like Jewish decisive victory (Deuteronomy 2:34). Make an example to deter others.
8:58وَإِمَّا تَخَافَنَّ مِنْ قَوْمٍ خِيَانَةً...Agar tujhe kisi qoum se khayanat ka khauf ho...If you fear treachery from a people...🚫 End Treacherous Treaties: Like Christian honesty (Matthew 5:37). Cancel agreements if betrayal is suspected.
8:59وَلَا يَحْسَبَنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا سَبَقُوا...Kafir yeh na samjhein ke woh bhaag gaye...Let not the disbelievers think they have escaped...⚖️ No Escape: Like Jewish inescapable judgment (Amos 9:1). Disbelievers cannot outrun God’s decree.
8:60وَأَعِدُّوا لَهُمْ مَا اسْتَطَعْتُمْ مِنْ قُوَّةٍ...Unke liye jitni taqat ho sake taiyar karo...Prepare against them whatever force you can...⚔️ Military Readiness: Like Jewish preparation (Nehemiah 4:9). Urges strength to deter enemies.
8:61وَإِنْ جَنَحُوا لِلسَّلْمِ فَاجْنَحْ لَهَا...Agar woh sulah ki taraf jhukte hain, toh tu bhi jhuk...If they incline to peace, incline to it...🕊️ Pursue Peace: Like Christian peacemaking (Matthew 5:9). Accept peace if offered sincerely.
8:62وَإِنْ يُرِيدُوا أَنْ يَخْدَعُوكَ فَإِنَّ حَسْبَكَ اللَّهُ...Agar woh tujhe dhoka dena chahein, toh Allah tera kaafi hai...If they intend to deceive you, Allah is sufficient for you...🛡️ God’s Sufficiency: Like Jewish trust in God (Psalm 20:7). God protects against deception.
8:63وَأَلَّفَ بَيْنَ قُلُوبِهِمْ...Usne unke dilon mein ulfat daali...He united their hearts...🤝 Divine Unity: Like Christian love binding believers (Colossians 3:14). God united Muslims’ hearts.
8:64يَا أَيُّهَا النَّبِيُّ حَسْبُكَ اللَّهُ...Aye Nabi, Allah tera aur mominon ka kaafi hai...O Prophet, Allah is sufficient for you and the believers...🌟 God’s Support: Like Jewish divine ally (Isaiah 41:10). God backs the Prophet and faithful.
8:65يَا أَيُّهَا النَّبِيُّ حَرِّضِ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ عَلَى الْقِتَالِ...Aye Nabi, mominon ko jang ke liye uksao...O Prophet, urge the believers to fight...⚔️ Encourage Jihad: Like Jewish call to battle (1 Samuel 17:32). Small faithful group can overcome many.
8:66الْآنَ خَفَّفَ اللَّهُ عَنْكُمْ...Ab Allah ne tum par se takleef halki ki...Now Allah has lightened your burden...🕊️ Eased Burden: Like Christian yoke lightened (Matthew 11:30). Concession for weaker faith; ten can face a hundred.
8:67مَا كَانَ لِنَبِيٍّ أَنْ يَكُونَ لَهُ أَسْرَىٰ...Kisi Nabi ke liye yeh munasib nahi ke uske paas qaidiyon ka izafa ho...It is not for a prophet to have captives until he has subdued...⚖️ War Ethics: Like Jewish laws of war (Deuteronomy 20:14). Captives acceptable only after victory.
8:68لَوْلَا كِتَابٌ مِنَ اللَّهِ سَبَقَ...Agar Allah ka pehle se likha hua na hota...Had it not been for a prior decree from Allah...🕊️ Divine Leniency: Like Christian mercy (Titus 3:5). God forgave early ransoming of captives.
8:69فَكُلُوا مِمَّا غَنِمْتُمْ حَلَالًا طَيِّبًا...Jo ghanimat tumne li, usse halaal aur paak samajh kar khao...Eat from what you have gained as lawful and good...🌟 Permitted Spoils: Like Jewish use of spoils (Numbers 31:9). Believers can use lawful gains.
8:70يَا أَيُّهَا النَّبِيُّ قُلْ لِمَنْ فِي أَيْدِيكُمْ مِنَ الْأَسْرَىٰ...Aye Nabi, jo qaidiyon ke paas hain, un se kaho...O Prophet, say to the captives in your hands...🕊️ Mercy to Captives: Like Christian kindness to enemies (Luke 6:35). Offer hope of ransom or release.
8:71وَإِنْ يُرِيدُوا خِيَانَةً فَقَدْ خَانُوا اللَّهَ...Agar woh khayanat ka irada karein, toh unhone Allah ke saath khayanat ki...If they intend treachery, they have betrayed Allah before...🚫 Treachery Condemned: Like Jewish betrayal’s fate (Psalm 55:23). Captives’ betrayal leads to ruin.
8:72إِنَّ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَهَاجَرُوا وَجَاهَدُوا...Jo imaan laaye, hijrat ki aur jang ki...Those who believed, migrated, and strove...🤝 Brotherhood: Like Christian mutual support (Galatians 6:2). Muhajirun and Ansar form a united community.
8:73وَالَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا بَعْضُهُمْ أَوْلِيَاءُ بَعْضٍ...Jo kafir hain, woh ek doosre ke dost hain...The disbelievers are allies of one another...😈 Enemy Alliance: Like Jewish united foes (Psalm 83:5). Disbelievers unite against believers.
8:74وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَهَاجَرُوا وَجَاهَدُوا...Jo imaan laaye, hijrat ki aur jang ki...Those who believed, migrated, and strove...🌴 Paradise for Strivers: Like Christian crown of life (Revelation 2:10). Faithful gain forgiveness and reward.
8:75وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا مِنْ بَعْدُ وَهَاجَرُوا...Jo baad mein imaan laaye aur hijrat ki...Those who believed afterward, migrated, and strove...🤝 Equal Status: Like Christian equality in faith (Galatians 3:28). Later believers share same rights as early ones.
