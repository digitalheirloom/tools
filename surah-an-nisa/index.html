Surah An-Nisa (Chapter 4: The Women)

Verses 1-100 Analysis 📖

📜 Introduction: Setting the Scene

  • Place and Context of Revelation

    • Historical Setting: Revealed in Medina after the Hijra, during a time of community-building and establishing justice.
    • Geographical Context: Medina, a diverse city with Muslims, Jews, Christians, and pagans, necessitating social guidelines.
    • Circumstances: Addresses family laws, inheritance, and social equity, reflecting the Quran’s practical and spiritual guidance.

  • 🔑 Key Themes and Topics

    • ✨ Social Justice: Rights of women, orphans, and the vulnerable.
    • 🧭 Family Laws: Marriage, divorce, and inheritance rules.
    • ⚖️ Ethical Conduct: Justice and honesty in dealings.
    • 🤝 Interfaith Relations: Guidance for interactions with People of the Book.

  • 🌍 Relevance Today

    • ⏳ Ethical Framework: Timeless principles for justice and equity.
    • 🤝 Interfaith Dialogue: Promotes understanding in diverse societies.
    • 💡 Social Reform: Offers insights for modern legal and welfare systems.

4:2وَآتُوا الْيَتَامَىٰ أَمْوَالَهُمْ...Aur yateemon ko unka maal do...Give the orphans their property...🤲 Protect Orphans: Echoes Judaism (Deuteronomy 24:17) and Buddhist compassion.
4:3وَإِنْ خِفْتُمْ أَلَّا تُقْسِطُوا فِي الْيَتَامَىٰ...Agar tum darte ho ki yateemon ke saath insaaf nahin karoge...If you fear that you will not deal justly with the orphans...⚖️ Justice in Marriage: Similar to Christian marital ethics (Ephesians 5:25).
4:4وَآتُوا النِّسَاءَ صَدُقَاتِهِنَّ نِحْلَةً...Aur aurton ko unka meher khushi se do...Give women their dowries graciously...💍 Dowry as Gift: Comparable to Jewish Ketubah and Hindu Stridhan.
4:5وَلَا تُؤْتُوا السُّفَهَاءَ أَمْوَالَكُمُ...Aur apna maal bewakoofon ko na do...Do not give the foolish your wealth...🧠 Financial Responsibility: Reflects Christian stewardship (Matthew 25:14-30).
4:6وَابْتَلُوا الْيَتَامَىٰ حَتَّىٰ إِذَا بَلَغُوا النِّكَاحَ...Aur yateemon ka imtihaan lo jab tak woh nikah ki umar ko na pahunch jayein...Test the orphans until they reach marriageable age...🧑‍⚖️ Guardianship: Similar to Hindu laws protecting minors’ assets.
4:7لِلرِّجَالِ نَصِيبٌ مِمَّا تَرَكَ الْوَالِدَانِ وَالْأَقْرَبُونَ...Mardon ke liye hissa hai usmein se jo maa-baap aur qareebi rishtedar chhod jayein...For men is a share of what parents and relatives leave...⚖️ Inheritance Rights: Structured like Judaism (Numbers 27:8-11).
4:8وَإِذَا حَضَرَ الْقِسْمَةَ أُولُو الْقُرْبَىٰ وَالْيَتَامَىٰ...Aur jab taqseem ke waqt qareebi rishtedar, yateem aur miskeen maujood hon...When relatives, orphans, and the needy are present at the division...🤝 Charity in Inheritance: Similar to Christian almsgiving (Luke 12:33).
4:9وَلْيَخْشَ الَّذِينَ لَوْ تَرَكُوا مِنْ خَلْفِهِمْ ذُرِّيَّةً ضِعَافًا...Aur darna chahiye un logon ko jo agar peeche chhod jayein kamzor aulaad...Let those fear who, if they left behind weak offspring...❤️ Empathy for Orphans: Echoes Buddhist compassion.
4:10إِنَّ الَّذِينَ يَأْكُلُونَ أَمْوَالَ الْيَتَامَىٰ ظُلْمًا...Jo log yateemon ka maal naahaq khaate hain...Those who consume orphans’ wealth unjustly...🔥 Divine Punishment: Similar to Christian warnings against greed (1 Timothy 6:10).
4:11يُوصِيكُمُ اللَّهُ فِي أَوْلَادِكُمْ...Allah tumhein tumhari aulaad ke baare mein wasiyat karta hai...Allah instructs you concerning your children...⚖️ Inheritance Laws: Like Jewish rules (Numbers 27).
4:12وَلَكُمْ نِصْفُ مَا تَرَكَ أَزْوَاجُكُمْ...Tumhare liye aadha hai jo tumhari biwiyan chhod jayein...For you is half of what your wives leave...💰 Spousal Inheritance: Reflects mutual responsibility in Hindu law.
4:13تِلْكَ حُدُودُ اللَّهِ...Yeh Allah ki hadein hain...These are the limits of Allah...🚧 Divine Boundaries: Similar to Torah’s legal limits.
4:14وَمَنْ يَعْصِ اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ...Jo Allah aur uske Rasool ki nafarmani kare...Whoever disobeys Allah and His Messenger...⚖️ Consequences: Echoes Christian obedience (John 14:15).
4:15وَاللَّاتِي يَأْتِينَ الْفَاحِشَةَ مِنْ نِسَائِكُمْ...Aur jo aurtein tum mein se badkaari karein...Those of your women who commit immorality...🧑‍⚖️ Legal Accountability: Reflects justice in Judaism (Leviticus 20).
4:16وَاللَّذَانِ يَأْتِيَانِهَا مِنْكُمْ فَآذُوهُمَا...Aur jo do mard us (badkaari) ko karein, unhein takleef do...If two men commit it, punish them both...⚖️ Equal Justice: Similar to Hindu Manu Smriti on punishment.
4:17إِنَّمَا التَّوْبَةُ عَلَى اللَّهِ لِلَّذِينَ يَعْمَلُونَ السُّوءَ بِجَهَالَةٍ...Allah ki taraf se tauba un logon ke liye hai jo jahalat mein bura kaam karein...Repentance is for those who do evil in ignorance...🙏 Repentance: Echoes Christian forgiveness (Luke 15:7).
4:18وَلَيْسَتِ التَّوْبَةُ لِلَّذِينَ يَعْمَلُونَ السَّيِّئَاتِ...Aur tauba un logon ke liye nahin jo bure kaam karte rahein...But repentance is not for those who persist in evil...⚠️ Insincere Repentance: Similar to Jewish Yom Kippur sincerity.
4:19يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا يَحِلُّ لَكُمْ أَنْ تَرِثُوا النِّسَاءَ كَرْهًا...Aye imaan walon, tumhare liye halal nahin ki aurton ko zabardasti waris banayein...O you who believe, it is not lawful for you to inherit women by force...🚫 Women’s Rights: Akin to Christian mutual respect (1 Peter 3:7).
4:20وَإِنْ أَرَدْتُمُ اسْتِبْدَالَ زَوْجٍ مَكَانَ زَوْجٍ...Aur agar tum ek biwi ki jagah doosri biwi rakhna chahte ho...If you want to replace one wife with another...💔 Divorce Ethics: Similar to Jewish Get fairness.
4:21وَكَيْفَ تَأْخُذُونَهُ وَقَدْ أَفْضَىٰ بَعْضُكُمْ إِلَىٰ بَعْضٍ...Aur kaise tum use wapas loge jab tum ek doosre ke saath mil chuke ho...How can you take it back after you have been intimate...❤️ Marital Bond: Echoes Christian marriage covenant (Malachi 2:14).
4:22وَلَا تَنْكِحُوا مَا نَكَحَ آبَاؤُكُمْ مِنَ النِّسَاءِ...Aur un aurton se nikah na karo jinse tumhare baap ne nikah kiya ho...Do not marry women your fathers married...🚫 Prohibited Marriages: Similar to Leviticus 18 in Judaism.
4:23حُرِّمَتْ عَلَيْكُمْ أُمَّهَاتُكُمْ وَبَنَاتُكُمْ...Tum par haram ki gayi hain tumhari maaein, betiyan...Prohibited to you are your mothers, daughters...🚫 Incest Taboo: Universal, e.g., Hinduism’s Sapinda rules.
4:24وَالْمُحْصَنَاتُ مِنَ النِّسَاءِ إِلَّا مَا مَلَكَتْ أَيْمَانُكُمْ...Aur shaadi-shuda aurton se nikah haram hai siwaye unke jo tumhari milk mein aayein...Forbidden are married women except those your right hands possess...⚖️ Marriage Laws: Historical context, interpreted variably today.
4:25وَمَنْ لَمْ يَسْتَطِعْ مِنْكُمْ طَوْلًا أَنْ يَنْكِحَ الْمُحْصَنَاتِ...Aur jo tum mein se azaad momina aurton se nikah ki taaqat na rakhta ho...Whoever cannot afford to marry free believing women...💍 Marriage Options: Echoes Christian widow remarriage (1 Timothy 5:14).
4:26يُرِيدُ اللَّهُ لِيُبَيِّنَ لَكُمْ وَيَهْدِيَكُمْ سُنَنَ الَّذِينَ مِنْ قَبْلِكُمْ...Allah chahta hai ki tumhein saaf bataye aur tumhein pehle walon ke tareeqe bataye...Allah wants to make clear to you and guide you to the ways of those before you...📜 Continuity of Guidance: Similar to Christian law fulfillment (Matthew 5:17).
4:27وَاللَّهُ يُرِيدُ أَنْ يَتُوبَ عَلَيْكُمْ...Aur Allah chahta hai ki tumhari tauba qubool kare...Allah wants to accept your repentance...🙏 Divine Mercy: Echoes Jewish forgiveness (Psalm 103:12).
4:28يُرِيدُ اللَّهُ أَنْ يُخَفِّفَ عَنْكُمْ...Allah chahta hai ki tum par se bojh halka kare...Allah wants to lighten your burden...🕊️ Ease in Religion: Similar to Christian “light yoke” (Matthew 11:30).
4:29يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَأْكُلُوا أَمْوَالَكُمْ بَيْنَكُمْ بِالْبَاطِلِ...Aye imaan walon, apna maal aapas mein naahaq mat khao...O you who believe, do not consume one another’s wealth unjustly...⚖️ Economic Justice: Reflects Hindu Dharma’s ethical trade.
4:30وَمَنْ يَفْعَلْ ذَٰلِكَ عُدْوَانًا وَظُلْمًا...Jo aisa karega baghawat aur zulm se...Whoever does that through aggression and injustice...🔥 Punishment for Injustice: Similar to Christian hell (Revelation 21:8).
4:31إِنْ تَجْتَنِبُوا كَبَائِرَ مَا تُنْهَوْنَ عَنْهُ...Agar tum bade gunaahon se bachte raho...If you avoid the major sins you are forbidden...🙏 Forgiveness for Minor Sins: Echoes Jewish minor atonement.
4:32وَلَا تَتَمَنَّوْا مَا فَضَّلَ اللَّهُ بِهِ بَعْضَكُمْ عَلَىٰ بَعْضٍ...Aur woh cheez mat chaho jismein Allah ne tum mein se baaz ko baaz par fazeelat di...Do not wish for what Allah has preferred some of you over others...😌 Contentment: Similar to Buddhist non-attachment.
4:33وَلِكُلٍّ جَعَلْنَا مَوَالِيَ مِمَّا تَرَكَ الْوَالِدَانِ...Aur har ek ke liye humne waris muqarrar kiye hain...For each, We have made heirs to what parents leave...⚖️ Inheritance Structure: Reflects Judaism’s laws.
4:34الرِّجَالُ قَوَّامُونَ عَلَى النِّسَاءِ...Mard aurton par nigraani karne wale hain...Men are in charge of women...👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Roles: Contextual; akin to Christian headship (Ephesians 5:23).
4:35وَإِنْ خِفْتُمْ شِقَاقَ بَيْنِهِمَا...Agar tumhein un dono ke darmiyaan na-ittifaqi ka khauf ho...If you fear dissension between them...🤝 Mediation: Similar to Jewish Beit Din.
4:36وَاعْبُدُوا اللَّهَ وَلَا تُشْرِكُوا بِهِ شَيْئًا...Allah ki ibadat karo aur uske saath kisi ko shareek na karo...Worship Allah and associate nothing with Him...☝️ Monotheism: Core in Judaism (Deuteronomy 6:4).
4:37الَّذِينَ يَبْخَلُونَ وَيَأْمُرُونَ النَّاسَ بِالْبُخْلِ...Jo log bakheel hain aur logon ko bhi bakheeli ka hukm dete hain...Those who are stingy and enjoin stinginess on others...🚫 Against Greed: Echoes Christian generosity (2 Corinthians 9:6).
4:38وَالَّذِينَ يُنْفِقُونَ أَمْوَالَهُمْ رِئَاءَ النَّاسِ...Aur jo log apna maal logon ko dikhane ke liye kharch karte hain...Those who spend their wealth to show off...🎭 Hypocrisy: Similar to Jesus’ critique (Matthew 6:2).
4:39وَمَاذَا عَلَيْهِمْ لَوْ آمَنُوا بِاللَّهِ...Un par kya harj tha agar woh Allah par imaan laate...What harm would it do them to believe in Allah...❓ Faith’s Benefit: Reflects Hindu Bhakti devotion.
4:40إِنَّ اللَّهَ لَا يَظْلِمُ مِثْقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ...Allah zarre bhar bhi zulm nahin karta...Allah does not do injustice, even as much as an atom’s weight...⚖️ Divine Justice: Echoes Buddhist karma.
4:41فَكَيْفَ إِذَا جِئْنَا مِنْ كُلِّ أُمَّةٍ بِشَهِيدٍ...Toh kaisa hoga jab hum har ummat se ek gawah laayeinge...How will it be when We bring from every nation a witness...👁️ Witnesses: Similar to Christian judgment (Revelation 20:12).

4:43يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَقْرَبُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَأَنْتُمْ سُكَارَىٰ...Aye imaan walon, namaz ke qareeb na jao jab tum nashe mein ho...O you who believe, do not approach prayer while intoxicated...🚫 Sobriety in Worship: Similar to Hindu ritual purity.

4:47يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ أُوتُوا الْكِتَابَ آمِنُوا...Aye Ahl-e-Kitab, imaan lao...O you who were given the Scripture, believe...🤝 Call to Faith: Similar to Christian Great Commission (Matthew 28:19).

4:52أُولَٰئِكَ الَّذِينَ لَعَنَهُمُ اللَّهُ...Yahi woh log hain jin par Allah ne laanat ki...Those are the ones whom Allah has cursed...⚖️ Divine Curse: Similar to biblical curses (Deuteronomy 28:15).
4:53أَمْ لَهُمْ نَصِيبٌ مِنَ الْمُلْكِ...Kya unka mulk mein koi hissa hai...Or do they have a share in the kingdom...👑 Kingdom of God: Echoes Christian concept (Matthew 5:3).
4:54أَمْ يَحْسُدُونَ النَّاسَ عَلَىٰ مَا آتَاهُمُ اللَّهُ...Ya woh logon se hasad karte hain us cheez par jo Allah ne unhein di...Or do they envy people for what Allah has given them...😒 Envy: Similar to Hindu Matsarya (jealousy).
4:55فَمِنْهُمْ مَنْ آمَنَ بِهِ وَمِنْهُمْ مَنْ صَدَّ عَنْهُ...Unmein se kuch us par imaan laaye aur kuch ne usse roka...Some believed in him, and some turned away...🤝 Division of Belief: Reflects Christian sower parable (Matthew 13:3-9).
4:56إِنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا بِآيَاتِنَا سَوْفَ نُصْلِيهِمْ نَارًا...Jo log hamari aayaton ka inkaar karte hain, hum unhein aag mein daalenge...Those who disbelieve in Our verses, We will drive them into a fire...🔥 Hellfire: Similar to Christian Gehenna (Matthew 13:42).
4:57وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ...Aur jo imaan laaye aur nek kaam kiye...But those who believe and do righteous deeds...🌟 Paradise: Echoes Hindu Swarga (heaven).
4:58إِنَّ اللَّهَ يَأْمُرُكُمْ أَنْ تُؤَدُّوا الْأَمَانَاتِ...Allah tumhein hukm deta hai ki amanatein unke haqdaaron ko do...Allah commands you to render trusts to whom they are due...🤲 Trustworthiness: Similar to Christian stewardship (1 Peter 4:10).
4:59يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا أَطِيعُوا اللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُوا الرَّسُولَ...Aye imaan walon, Allah ki itaat karo aur Rasool ki itaat karo...O you who believe, obey Allah and obey the Messenger...🙇 Obedience: Reflects Jewish obedience to God’s law.
4:60أَلَمْ تَرَ إِلَى الَّذِينَ يَزْعُمُونَ أَنَّهُمْ آمَنُوا...Kya tumne un logon ko nahin dekha jo daawa karte hain ki woh imaan laaye...Have you not seen those who claim to believe...🎭 Hypocrisy: Similar to Christian warnings (Matthew 7:21-23).

4:63أُولَٰئِكَ الَّذِينَ يَعْلَمُ اللَّهُ مَا فِي قُلُوبِهِمْ...Yahi woh log hain jinke dilon mein jo hai Allah jaanta hai...Those are the ones whom Allah knows what is in their hearts...👁️ Omniscience: Reflects Christian God’s knowledge (Hebrews 4:13).

  • Place and Context of Revelation

    • Circumstances: Verses 101-176 address practical issues like prayer during travel or conflict, hypocrisy, family laws, and interfaith relations, reflecting the Quran’s role as both spiritual and societal guidance.

    • 🛡️ Guidance in Conflict: Instructions for prayer and vigilance during travel or war, emphasizing trust in God.
    • ⚖️ Social Justice: Laws on inheritance, marriage, and protecting the vulnerable (e.g., orphans, women).
    • 🧭 Combating Hypocrisy: Warnings against those who undermine the community with insincere faith.
    • 🤝 Interfaith Dynamics: Engaging with the People of the Book (Jews and Christians) while affirming monotheism.

  • 🌍 Relevance Today

    • ⏳ Timeless Principles: Offers ethical frameworks for justice, community cohesion, and resilience in adversity.
    • 🤝 Interfaith Dialogue: Encourages mutual respect and understanding in pluralistic societies.
    • 💡 Modern Application: Provides insights for legal systems, conflict resolution, and social welfare.

4:131وَلِلَّهِ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ...Allah ke liye hai jo aasmanon aur zameen mein hai...To Allah belongs whatever is in the heavens and earth...🌍 God’s Dominion: Reflects Christian Lord’s Prayer (Matthew 6:10).
4:132وَلِلَّهِ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ...Allah ke liye hai jo aasmanon aur zameen mein hai...To Allah belongs whatever is in the heavens and earth...🌍 Reaffirmed Sovereignty: Echoes Psalm 24:1 (Judaism/Christianity).
4:133إِنْ يَشَأْ يُذْهِبْكُمْ أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ...Agar woh chahe toh tum logon ko hata de...If He wills, He can remove you, O people...⚖️ Divine Power: Similar to Hindu Vishnu’s cosmic authority.
4:134مَنْ كَانَ يُرِيدُ ثَوَابَ الدُّنْيَا...Jo koi duniya ka sawab chahta hai...Whoever desires the reward of this world...🌍 Worldly vs. Eternal: Echoes Christian eternal focus (Matthew 6:19-21).
4:135يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا كُونُوا قَوَّامِينَ بِالْقِسْطِ...Aye imaan walon, insaaf ke saath qaaim raho...O you who believe, be steadfast in justice...⚖️ Justice: Similar to Jewish “justice, justice” (Deuteronomy 16:20).
4:136يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا آمِنُوا بِاللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِ...Aye imaan walon, Allah aur uske Rasool par imaan lao...O you who believe, believe in Allah and His Messenger...🙏 Firm Faith: Echoes Christian steadfastness (Hebrews 11:6).
4:137إِنَّ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا ثُمَّ كَفَرُوا ثُمَّ آمَنُوا ثُمَّ كَفَرُوا...Jo imaan laaye phir kafir hue, phir imaan laaye phir kafir hue...Those who believed, then disbelieved, then believed, then disbelieved...⚠️ Apostasy: Similar to Christian backsliding warnings (Hebrews 10:26).
4:138بَشِّرِ الْمُنَافِقِينَ بِأَنَّ لَهُمْ عَذَابًا أَلِيمًا...Munafiqon ko khabar de ki unke liye dardnaak azaab hai...Give tidings to the hypocrites of a painful punishment...🔥 Hypocrisy’s Consequence: Echoes Christian false disciples (Matthew 7:21-23).
4:139الَّذِينَ يَتَّخِذُونَ الْكَافِرِينَ أَوْلِيَاءَ مِنْ دُونِ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ...Jo kafiron ko momineen ke bajaaye dost banate hain...Those who take disbelievers as allies instead of believers...🤝 Loyalty to Believers: Reflects Jewish communal solidarity.
4:140وَقَدْ نَزَّلَ عَلَيْكُمْ فِي الْكِتَابِ أَنْ إِذَا سَمِعْتُمْ آيَاتِ اللَّهِ...Kitaab mein tum par naazil kiya gaya ki jab tum Allah ki aayaton ka mazaaq suno...It has been revealed to you in the Book that when you hear Allah’s verses mocked...🚫 Avoid Mockery: Similar to Christian respect for scripture (2 Timothy 3:16).
4:141الَّذِينَ يَتَرَبَّصُونَ بِكُمْ...Jo tumhare baare mein intezaar karte hain...Those who wait and watch you...👁️ Hypocritical Opportunism: Echoes Hindu warnings against deceit.
4:142إِنَّ الْمُنَافِقِينَ يُخَادِعُونَ اللَّهَ...Munafiqeen Allah ko dhoka dete hain...The hypocrites try to deceive Allah...🎭 Deceptive Faith: Similar to Christian wolves in sheep’s clothing (Matthew 7:15).
4:143مُذَبْذَبِينَ بَيْنَ ذَٰلِكَ لَا إِلَىٰ هَٰؤُلَاءِ وَلَا إِلَىٰ هَٰؤُلَاءِ...Woh idhar udhar dolte hain, na inke saath na unke saath...Wavering between them, neither with these nor those...🤔 Indecision: Reflects Christian “double-minded” warning (James 1:8).
4:144يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَتَّخِذُوا الْكَافِرِينَ أَوْلِيَاءَ...Aye imaan walon, kafiron ko dost na banao...O you who believe, do not take disbelievers as allies...🤝 Community Loyalty: Similar to Jewish covenantal exclusivity.
4:145إِنَّ الْمُنَافِقِينَ فِي الدَّرْكِ الْأَسْفَلِ مِنَ النَّارِ...Munafiqeen aag ke sabse neeche darje mein honge...The hypocrites will be in the lowest depths of the Fire...🔥 Lowest Hell: Echoes Christian outer darkness (Matthew 25:30).
4:146إِلَّا الَّذِينَ تَابُوا وَأَصْلَحُوا وَاعْتَصَمُوا بِاللَّهِ...Siwaye un logon ke jo tauba karein aur sudhar karein aur Allah ko mazbooti se pakdein...Except those who repent, reform, and hold fast to Allah...🙏 Redemption: Similar to Jewish Teshuvah and Christian repentance (Acts 3:19).
4:147مَا يَفْعَلُ اللَّهُ بِعَذَابِكُمْ إِنْ شَكَرْتُمْ وَآمَنْتُمْ...Allah kyun tumhein saza de agar tum shukr karo aur imaan lao...Why would Allah punish you if you are grateful and believe...🌟 Gratitude and Faith: Echoes Hindu Bhakti (devotion).
4:148لَا يُحِبُّ اللَّهُ الْجَهْرَ بِالسُّوءِ مِنَ الْقَوْلِ...Allah buraai ka zaaher karna pasand nahin karta...Allah does not like the public mention of evil...🤫 Avoid Slander: Similar to Christian tongue control (James 3:6).
4:149إِنْ تُبْدُوا خَيْرًا أَوْ تُخْفُوهُ أَوْ تَعْفُوا عَنْ سُوءٍ...Agar tum khair ko zaahir karo ya chhupao ya buraai maaf karo...If you disclose good or conceal it or pardon evil...🤝 Forgiveness: Echoes Christian “forgive us our debts” (Matthew 6:12).
4:150إِنَّ الَّذِينَ يَكْفُرُونَ بِاللَّهِ وَرُسُلِهِ...Jo Allah aur uske Rasoolon ka inkaar karte hain...Those who disbelieve in Allah and His messengers...🚫 Rejection of Prophets: Similar to Jewish rejection warnings (Isaiah 6:9-10).
4:151أُولَٰئِكَ هُمُ الْكَافِرُونَ حَقًّا...Yahi woh log hain jo sach-much kafir hain...Those are the true disbelievers...⚖️ True Disbelief: Echoes Christian denial of Christ (1 John 2:22).
4:152وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا بِاللَّهِ وَرُسُلِهِ...Aur jo Allah aur uske Rasoolon par imaan laaye...But those who believe in Allah and His messengers...🌟 Reward for Faith: Similar to Hindu Punya (merit).
4:153يَسْأَلُكَ أَهْلُ الْكِتَابِ أَنْ تُنَزِّلَ عَلَيْهِمْ كِتَابًا...Ahl-e-Kitaab tujhse maangte hain ki tu un par aasmaan se Kitaab utare...The People of the Book ask you to bring down a book from heaven...📜 Demand for Signs: Echoes Christian Pharisees’ demands (Matthew 12:38).
4:154وَرَفَعْنَا فَوْقَهُمُ الطُّورَ بِمِيثَاقِهِمْ...Aur humne unke oopar Toor ko unke ahd ke saath uthaya...We raised the Mount over them for their covenant...🏔️ Covenant: Similar to Jewish Sinai covenant (Exodus 19:5).
4:155فَبِمَا نَقْضِهِمْ مِيثَاقَهُمْ...Unke ahd torne ke wajah se...Because they broke their covenant...⚖️ Broken Covenant: Echoes biblical consequences (Deuteronomy 28:15).
4:156وَبِكُفْرِهِمْ وَقَوْلِهِمْ عَلَىٰ مَرْيَمَ بُهْتَانًا عَظِيمًا...Aur unke kufr aur Maryam par bada ilzaam lagane ke wajah se...For their disbelief and their saying against Mary a great slander...🚫 Slander Against Mary: Clarifies Christian narrative (Luke 1:35).
4:157وَقَوْلِهِمْ إِنَّا قَتَلْنَا الْمَسِيحَ عِيسَى ابْنَ مَرْيَمَ...Aur unke kehne se ki humne Masih Isa ibn Maryam ko qatl kiya...And their saying, “We killed the Messiah, Jesus, son of Mary...”✝️ Crucifixion Denial: Differs from Christian belief (John 19:30).
4:158بَلْ رَفَعَهُ اللَّهُ إِلَيْهِ...Balki Allah ne usse apni taraf uthaya...Rather, Allah raised him to Himself...⬆️ Ascension: Parallels Christian Ascension (Acts 1:9), with differences.
4:159وَإِنْ مِنْ أَهْلِ الْكِتَابِ إِلَّا لَيُؤْمِنَنَّ بِهِ قَبْلَ مَوْتِهِ...Aur Ahl-e-Kitaab mein se koi aisa nahin jo us par imaan na laye uski maut se pehle...There is none from the People of the Book but will believe in him before his death...🤝 Future Belief: Unique, with parallels to Christian Second Coming (Revelation 1:7).
4:160فَبِظُلْمٍ مِنَ الَّذِينَ هَادُوا حَرَّمْنَا عَلَيْهِمْ طَيِّبَاتٍ...Yahudon ke zulm ke wajah se humne un par pak cheezein haram ki...For the wrongdoing of the Jews, We made unlawful for them pure things...🚫 Divine Restrictions: Similar to Jewish dietary laws (Leviticus 11).
4:161وَأَخْذِهِمُ الرِّبَا وَقَدْ نُهُوا عَنْهُ...Aur unke sood lene ke wajah se jabki unhein mana kiya gaya tha...And for their taking usury while they were forbidden...💰 Against Usury: Echoes Jewish prohibition (Leviticus 25:36).
4:162لَٰكِنِ الرَّاسِخُونَ فِي الْعِلْمِ مِنْهُمْ...Lekin unmein se ilm mein pakke log...But those firm in knowledge among them...🌟 Righteous Scholars: Similar to Christian “scribe for the kingdom” (Matthew 13:52).
4:163إِنَّا أَوْحَيْنَا إِلَيْكَ كَمَا أَوْحَيْنَا إِلَىٰ نُوحٍ...Humne tujh par wahi ki jaise Nuh par ki...We have revealed to you as We revealed to Noah...📜 Prophetic Continuity: Echoes Jewish prophetic tradition (Isaiah 1:1).
4:164وَرُسُلًا قَدْ قَصَصْنَاهُمْ عَلَيْكَ مِنْ قَبْلُ...Aur Rasoolon ke baare mein humne tujh par pehle qisse bayan kiye...And messengers We have mentioned to you before...🌟 Prophets: Similar to Christian Old Testament prophets (Hebrews 1:1).
4:165رُسُلًا مُبَشِّرِينَ وَمُنْذِرِينَ...Rasool khushkhabri dene wale aur khatra batane wale...Messengers as bearers of good tidings and warners...🕊️ Prophetic Mission: Echoes Hindu Rishi warnings.
4:166لَٰكِنِ اللَّهُ يَشْهَدُ بِمَا أَنْزَلَ إِلَيْكَ...Lekin Allah uski gawahi deta hai jo usne tujh par naazil kiya...But Allah bears witness to what He revealed to you...👁️ Divine Testimony: Similar to Christian Spirit’s testimony (John 15:26).
4:167إِنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا وَصَدُّوا عَنْ سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ...Jo kafir hue aur Allah ki raah se roka...Those who disbelieve and obstruct Allah’s path...⚠️ Obstruction: Echoes Christian stumbling block (1 Corinthians 1:23).
4:168إِنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا وَظَلَمُوا لَمْ يَكُنِ اللَّهُ لِيَغْفِرَ لَهُمْ...Jo kafir hue aur zulm kiya, Allah unhein maaf nahin karega...Those who disbelieve and do wrong, Allah will not forgive them...🔥 Unforgiven: Similar to Christian unpardonable sin (Matthew 12:31).
4:169إِلَّا طَرِيقَ جَهَنَّمَ خَالِدِينَ فِيهَا أَبَدًا...Siwaye Jahannam ke raaste ke, jahan woh hamesha rahenge...Except the way to Hell, abiding therein forever...🔥 Eternal Punishment: Echoes Hindu Naraka (hell).
4:170يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ قَدْ جَاءَكُمُ الرَّسُولُ بِالْحَقِّ...Aye logo, Rasool tumhare paas haq ke saath aaya hai...O mankind, the Messenger has come to you with the truth...📜 Truthful Message: Similar to Christian gospel (John 14:6).
4:171يَا أَهْلَ الْكِتَابِ لَا تَغْلُوا فِي دِينِكُمْ...Aye Ahl-e-Kitaab, apne deen mein hadd se na guzro...O People of the Book, do not exaggerate in your religion...⚖️ Moderation: Corrects Christian Trinity concept, aligns with Jewish monotheism.
4:172لَنْ يَسْتَنْكِفَ الْمَسِيحُ أَنْ يَكُونَ عَبْدًا لِلَّهِ...Masih kabhi inkaar nahin karega ki woh Allah ka banda ho...The Messiah will never disdain to be a servant of Allah...🙏 Humility of Jesus: Differs from Christian divinity (John 1:1).
4:173فَأَمَّا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ...Jo imaan laaye aur nek kaam kiye...As for those who believe and do righteous deeds...🌟 Reward for Righteousness: Echoes Hindu Punya (merit).
4:174يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ قَدْ جَاءَكُمْ بُرْهَانٌ مِنْ رَبِّكُمْ...Aye logo, tumhare Rab ki taraf se burhaan aaya hai...O mankind, a proof has come to you from your Lord...📜 Divine Proof: Similar to Christian fulfilled prophecy (Isaiah 7:14).
4:175فَأَمَّا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا بِاللَّهِ وَاعْتَصَمُوا بِهِ...Jo Allah par imaan laaye aur usse mazbooti se pakde...As for those who believe in Allah and hold fast to Him...🌴 Guidance and Reward: Echoes Christian “abide in me” (John 15:4).
4:176يَسْتَفْتُونَكَ قُلِ اللَّهُ يُفْتِيكُمْ فِي الْكَلَالَةِ...Woh tujhse fatwa maangte hain, kaho Allah tumhein kalala ke baare mein fatwa deta hai...They ask you for a ruling. Say, “Allah gives you a ruling about kalala...”⚖️ Inheritance Clarification: Similar to Jewish succession laws (Numbers 27:8-11).
