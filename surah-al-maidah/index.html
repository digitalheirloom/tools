Surah Al-Ma’idah (Chapter 5: The Table Spread) - Verses 1-120 Analysis 📖

📜 Introduction: Setting the Scene

  • Place and Context of Revelation

    • Historical Setting: Revealed in Medina, primarily after the Treaty of Hudaybiyyah (6 AH/628 CE) and during the Farewell Pilgrimage (10 AH/632 CE), marking the near-completion of Islamic law and community consolidation.
    • Geographical Context: Medina, a diverse city with Muslims, Jews, Christians, and pagans, necessitating clear legal and interfaith guidelines.
    • Circumstances: Surah Al-Ma’idah addresses covenants, dietary laws, justice, interfaith relations, and the finality of Prophethood, serving as a “constitution” for the Muslim community and a call to uphold commitments.

  • 🔑 Key Themes and Topics

    • 🤝 Fulfillment of Covenants: Emphasis on honoring contracts and divine obligations.
    • ⚖️ Justice and Law: Detailed rulings on diet, marriage, punishment, and fairness.
    • 🧭 Interfaith Dialogue: Engagement with Jews and Christians, affirming monotheism while correcting theological deviations.
    • ✨ Finality of Guidance: Completion of Islam as a perfected religion.

  • 🌍 Relevance Today

    • ⏳ Ethical Framework: Timeless principles for justice, integrity, and coexistence.
    • 🤝 Interfaith Understanding: Encourages dialogue and mutual respect in pluralistic societies.
    • 💡 Social Governance: Offers insights for legal systems, community ethics, and personal responsibility.

5:1يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا أَوْفُوا بِالْعُقُودِ...Aye imaan walon, apne ahd poore karo...O you who believe, fulfill [all] contracts...🤝 Honor Covenants: Similar to Jewish covenant with God (Exodus 19:5).
5:2يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تُحِلُّوا شَعَائِرَ اللَّهِ...Aye imaan walon, Allah ke nishaniyon ko halal na karo...O you who believe, do not violate the rites of Allah...🕋 Respect Sacred Rites: Echoes Hindu reverence for rituals.
5:3حُرِّمَتْ عَلَيْكُمُ الْمَيْتَةُ وَالدَّمُ وَلَحْمُ الْخِنْزِيرِ...Tum par haram ki gayi murdaar, khoon, aur suar ka gosht...Prohibited to you are dead animals, blood, and the flesh of swine...🚫 Dietary Laws: Similar to Jewish kosher laws (Leviticus 11).
5:4يَسْأَلُونَكَ مَاذَا أُحِلَّ لَهُمْ...Woh tujhse poochhte hain ki unke liye kya halal kiya gaya...They ask you what is lawful for them...🍽️ Permitted Foods: Reflects Christian dietary freedom (Acts 10:15).
5:5الْيَوْمَ أُحِلَّ لَكُمُ الطَّيِّبَاتُ...Aaj tumhare liye paak cheezein halal ki gayi...This day [all] good foods have been made lawful...🤝 Interfaith Marriage: Allows marriage with People of the Book, akin to Jewish intermarriage debates.
5:6يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِذَا قُمْتُمْ إِلَى الصَّلَاةِ...Aye imaan walon, jab tum namaz ke liye utho...O you who believe, when you rise for prayer...🧼 Ritual Purity: Similar to Hindu Snana (bathing before worship).
5:7وَاذْكُرُوا نِعْمَةَ اللَّهِ عَلَيْكُمْ وَمِيثَاقَهُ...Aur Allah ki ne’mat aur uske ahd ko yaad karo...Remember Allah’s favor upon you and His covenant...🙏 Gratitude for Covenant: Echoes Christian grace (Ephesians 2:8).
5:8يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا كُونُوا قَوَّامِينَ لِلَّهِ...Aye imaan walon, Allah ke liye insaaf ke saath qaaim raho...O you who believe, be steadfast for Allah, witnesses in justice...⚖️ Justice: Similar to Jewish “justice, justice” (Deuteronomy 16:20).
5:9وَعَدَ اللَّهُ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ...Allah ne wada kiya jo imaan laaye aur nek kaam kiye...Allah has promised those who believe and do righteous deeds...🌟 Reward for Faith: Echoes Hindu Punya (merit).
5:10وَالَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا وَكَذَّبُوا بِآيَاتِنَا...Aur jo kafir hue aur hamari aayaton ko jhuthlaya...Those who disbelieve and deny Our signs...🔥 Punishment: Similar to Christian hell (Revelation 21:8).
5:11يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا اذْكُرُوا نِعْمَتَ اللَّهِ عَلَيْكُمْ...Aye imaan walon, Allah ki ne’mat ko yaad karo...O you who believe, remember Allah’s favor upon you...🛡️ Divine Protection: Reflects Psalm 91 (Judaism/Christianity).
5:12وَلَقَدْ أَخَذَ اللَّهُ مِيثَاقَ بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ...Allah ne Bani Israeel se ahd liya...Allah took a covenant from the Children of Israel...🤝 Israel’s Covenant: Echoes Exodus 24 (Judaism).
5:13فَبِمَا نَقْضِهِمْ مِيثَاقَهُمْ لَعَنَّاهُمْ...Unke ahd torne ke wajah se humne un par laanat ki...Because they broke their covenant, We cursed them...⚖️ Broken Covenant: Similar to Deuteronomy 28:15 (Judaism).
5:14وَمِنَ الَّذِينَ قَالُوا إِنَّا نَصَارَىٰ أَخَذْنَا مِيثَاقَهُمْ...Aur jin logon ne kaha hum Nasara hain, humne unka ahd liya...From those who said, “We are Christians,” We took their covenant...✝️ Christian Covenant: Reflects Christian New Covenant (Hebrews 8:8).
5:15يَا أَهْلَ الْكِتَابِ قَدْ جَاءَكُمْ رَسُولُنَا...Aye Ahl-e-Kitaab, hamara Rasool tumhare paas aaya...O People of the Book, Our Messenger has come to you...📜 Prophetic Message: Similar to Christian gospel call (Matthew 28:19).
5:16يَهْدِي بِهِ اللَّهُ مَنِ اتَّبَعَ رِضْوَانَهُ...Allah uske zariye hidayat deta hai jo uski raza ko apnata hai...Allah guides those who seek His approval...🧭 Guidance: Echoes Christian “narrow path” (Matthew 7:14).
5:17لَقَدْ كَفَرَ الَّذِينَ قَالُوا إِنَّ اللَّهَ هُوَ الْمَسِيحُ...Kafir hue jo bole ki Allah Masih hai...They disbelieve who say, “Allah is the Messiah...”☝️ Monotheism: Corrects Christian Trinity, aligns with Jewish Shema (Deuteronomy 6:4).
5:18وَقَالَتِ الْيَهُودُ وَالنَّصَارَىٰ نَحْنُ أَبْنَاءُ اللَّهِ...Yahudi aur Nasara bole, “Hum Allah ke bete hain...”The Jews and Christians say, “We are the children of Allah...”🚫 No Divine Sonship: Differs from Christian “children of God” (John 1:12).
5:19يَا أَهْلَ الْكِتَابِ قَدْ جَاءَكُمْ رَسُولُنَا يُبَيِّنُ لَكُمْ...Aye Ahl-e-Kitaab, hamara Rasool tumhein saaf batane aaya...O People of the Book, Our Messenger has come to clarify to you...📜 Clarification: Similar to Christian fulfilled prophecy (Luke 24:44).
5:20وَإِذْ قَالَ مُوسَىٰ لِقَوْمِهِ...Aur jab Moosa ne apni qoum se kaha...When Moses said to his people...🧑‍⚖️ Moses’ Leadership: Echoes Exodus 19 (Judaism).
5:21يَا قَوْمِ ادْخُلُوا الْأَرْضَ الْمُقَدَّسَةَ...Aye meri qoum, muqaddas zameen mein dakhil ho...O my people, enter the Holy Land...🕍 Holy Land: Reflects Jewish Promised Land (Deuteronomy 1:8).
5:22قَالُوا يَا مُوسَىٰ إِنَّ فِيهَا قَوْمًا جَبَّارِينَ...Unhone kaha, “Aye Moosa, wahan zabardast log hain...”They said, “O Moses, there are mighty people there...”😨 Fear of Giants: Echoes Numbers 13:32-33 (Judaism).
5:23قَالَ رَجُلَانِ مِنَ الَّذِينَ يَخَافُونَ...Do mardon ne kaha jo darte the...Two men who feared [Allah] said...🛡️ Faithful Courage: Similar to Joshua and Caleb (Numbers 14:6-9).
5:24قَالُوا يَا مُوسَىٰ إِنَّا لَنْ نَدْخُلَهَا أَبَدًا...Unhone kaha, “Aye Moosa, hum kabhi wahan nahin jayenge...”They said, “O Moses, we will never enter it...”🚫 Refusal: Reflects Jewish rebellion (Numbers 14:1-4).
5:25قَالَ رَبِّ إِنِّي لَا أَمْلِكُ إِلَّا نَفْسِي وَأَخِي...Usne kaha, “Aye mere Rab, mere bas mein sirf meri jaan aur mera bhai hai...”He said, “My Lord, I control only myself and my brother...”🙏 Moses’ Plea: Echoes Exodus 32:11 (Judaism).
5:26قَالَ فَإِنَّهَا مُحَرَّمَةٌ عَلَيْهِمْ أَرْبَعِينَ سَنَةً...Allah ne kaha, “Yeh zameen un par chalees saal ke liye haram hai...”Allah said, “It is forbidden to them for forty years...”🏜️ Wandering Punishment: Reflects Numbers 14:33-34 (Judaism).
5:27وَاتْلُ عَلَيْهِمْ نَبَأَ ابْنَيْ آدَمَ بِالْحَقِّ...Aur unhein dono bete Adam ke qisse ko haq ke saath padh kar suna...Recite to them the story of the two sons of Adam in truth...⚖️ Cain and Abel: Echoes Genesis 4 (Judaism/Christianity).
5:28لَئِنْ بَسَطْتَ إِلَيَّ يَدَكَ لِتَقْتُلَنِي...Agar tu mujhe qatl karne ke liye haath uthaye...If you raise your hand to kill me...🕊️ Nonviolence: Similar to Christian “turn the other cheek” (Matthew 5:39).
5:29إِنِّي أُرِيدُ أَنْ تَبُوءَ بِإِثْمِي وَإِثْمِكَ...Main chahta hoon ki tu mere aur apne gunaah ka bojh uthaye...I want you to bear my sin and your sin...⚖️ Moral Responsibility: Reflects Hindu karma.
5:30فَطَوَّعَتْ لَهُ نَفْسُهُ قَتْلَ أَخِيهِ...Uske nafs ne usse apne bhai ke qatl ke liye razi kiya...His soul prompted him to murder his brother...😈 Evil Impulse: Echoes Christian sinful nature (Romans 7:18).
5:31فَبَعَثَ اللَّهُ غُرَابًا يَبْحَثُ فِي الْأَرْضِ...Phir Allah ne ek kauwa bheja jo zameen khod raha tha...Allah sent a crow digging in the ground...🐦 Divine Lesson: Unique, but parallels biblical teaching through creation (Job 12:7-10).
5:32مِنْ أَجْلِ ذَٰلِكَ كَتَبْنَا عَلَىٰ بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ...Is wajah se humne Bani Israeel par likha...We decreed upon the Children of Israel...⚖️ Value of Life: Echoes Jewish “whoever saves a life” (Talmud, Sanhedrin 37a).
5:33إِنَّمَا جَزَاءُ الَّذِينَ يُحَارِبُونَ اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ...Jo Allah aur uske Rasool se jung karte hain unka badla...The penalty for those who wage war against Allah and His Messenger...⚖️ Punishment for Rebellion: Similar to biblical laws against rebellion (Deuteronomy 17:12).
5:34إِلَّا الَّذِينَ تَابُوا مِنْ قَبْلِ أَنْ تَقْدِرُوا عَلَيْهِمْ...Siwaye un logon ke jo tauba kar lein isse pehle ki tum un par qaboo pao...Except those who repent before you apprehend them...🙏 Repentance: Echoes Christian forgiveness (Luke 15:7).
5:35يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا اتَّقُوا اللَّهَ...Aye imaan walon, Allah se daro...O you who believe, fear Allah...🙏 God-Consciousness: Similar to Hindu Dharma (righteousness).
5:36إِنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا لَوْ أَنَّ لَهُمْ مَا فِي الْأَرْضِ...Jo kafir hue, agar unke paas zameen ki saari cheezein hon...Those who disbelieve, if they had everything on earth...💰 Futility of Wealth: Echoes Christian rich fool (Luke 12:16-21).
5:37يُرِيدُونَ أَنْ يَخْرُجُوا مِنَ النَّارِ وَمَا هُمْ بِخَارِجِينَ...Woh aag se nikalna chahte hain lekin nikal nahin sakte...They will want to get out of the Fire but will not...🔥 Eternal Fire: Similar to Hindu Naraka (hell).
5:38وَالسَّارِقُ وَالسَّارِقَةُ فَاقْطَعُوا أَيْدِيَهُمَا...Choron aur choriyon ke haath kaat do...As for the thief, male or female, cut off their hands...⚖️ Theft Punishment: Contextual; parallels biblical restitution (Exodus 22:1).
5:39فَمَنْ تَابَ مِنْ بَعْدِ ظُلْمِهِ وَأَصْلَحَ...Jo apne zulm ke baad tauba kare aur sudhar kare...But whoever repents after his wrongdoing and reforms...🙏 Reformation: Echoes Jewish Teshuvah (repentance).
5:40أَلَمْ تَعْلَمْ أَنَّ اللَّهَ لَهُ مُلْكُ السَّمَاوَاتِ...Kya tu nahin jaanta ki Allah ke liye aasmanon ka mulk hai...Do you not know that to Allah belongs the dominion of the heavens...🌍 Divine Sovereignty: Echoes Psalm 24:1 (Judaism/Christianity).
5:41يَا أَيُّهَا الرَّسُولُ لَا يَحْزُنْكَ الَّذِينَ يُسَارِعُونَ فِي الْكُفْرِ...Aye Rasool, un logon se dilgir na ho jo kufr mein jaldi karte hain...O Messenger, do not be grieved by those who hasten into disbelief...😔 Prophet’s Patience: Similar to Christian endurance (2 Timothy 2:24).
5:42سَمَّاعُونَ لِلْكَذِبِ أَكَّالُونَ لِلسُّحْتِ...Woh jhoot ke liye kaan lagate hain, haraam ke khane wale...Listeners to falsehood, devourers of the unlawful...🚫 Corruption: Echoes Christian false teachers (2 Peter 2:3).
5:43وَكَيْفَ يُحَكِّمُونَكَ وَعِنْدَهُمُ التَّوْرَاةُ...Aur kaise woh tujhe faisla karne ke liye bulate hain jab unke paas Taurat hai...How do they make you their judge when they have the Torah...📜 Scriptural Neglect: Similar to Christian scripture misuse (2 Timothy 2:15).
5:44إِنَّا أَنْزَلْنَا التَّوْرَاةَ فِيهَا هُدًى وَنُورٌ...Humne Taurat naazil ki jismein hidayat aur noor hai...We revealed the Torah, in which is guidance and light...📜 Torah’s Guidance: Echoes Jewish reverence for Torah (Psalm 119:105).
5:45وَكَتَبْنَا عَلَيْهِمْ فِيهَا أَنَّ النَّفْسَ بِالنَّفْسِ...Aur humne un par likha ki jaan ke badle jaan...We prescribed for them: a life for a life...⚖️ Retribution: Reflects Jewish “eye for an eye” (Exodus 21:24).
5:46وَقَفَّيْنَا عَلَىٰ آثَارِهِمْ بِعِيسَى ابْنِ مَرْيَمَ...Aur humne unke peeche Isa ibn Maryam ko bheja...We sent Jesus, son of Mary, following them...✝️ Jesus’ Mission: Echoes Christian Gospel (Matthew 5:17).
5:47وَلْيَحْكُمْ أَهْلُ الْإِنْجِيلِ بِمَا أَنْزَلَ اللَّهُ فِيهِ...Ahl-e-Injeel uske zariye hukm karein jo Allah ne naazil kiya...Let the people of the Gospel judge by what Allah revealed...📜 Gospel Authority: Reflects Christian adherence to Jesus’ teachings (John 12:48).
5:48وَأَنْزَلْنَا إِلَيْكَ الْكِتَابَ بِالْحَقِّ...Aur humne tujh par Kitaab haq ke saath naazil ki...We revealed to you the Book in truth...📖 Quran’s Role: Unique, but parallels Christian scripture fulfillment (Hebrews 1:1-2).
5:49وَأَنِ احْكُمْ بَيْنَهُمْ بِمَا أَنْزَلَ اللَّهُ...Aur unke darmiyaan uske zariye hukm kar jo Allah ne naazil kiya...Judge between them by what Allah has revealed...⚖️ Divine Judgment: Similar to Jewish rabbinical courts.
5:50أَفَحُكْمَ الْجَاهِلِيَّةِ يَبْغُونَ...Kya woh jahiliyyat ka hukm talab karte hain...Do they seek the judgment of ignorance...🚫 Reject Ignorance: Echoes Christian renewal of mind (Romans 12:2).
5:51يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَتَّخِذُوا الْيَهُودَ وَالنَّصَارَىٰ أَوْلِيَاءَ...Aye imaan walon, Yahudi aur Nasara ko dost na banao...O you who believe, do not take Jews and Christians as allies...🤝 Community Loyalty: Contextual; reflects Jewish covenantal exclusivity.
5:52فَتَرَى الَّذِينَ فِي قُلُوبِهِمْ مَرَضٌ...Tu dekhega jin ke dilon mein bimari hai...You see those with disease in their hearts...🎭 Hypocrisy: Similar to Christian false believers (Matthew 7:21-23).
5:53وَيَقُولُ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا أَهَٰؤُلَاءِ الَّذِينَ أَقْسَمُوا...Aur imaan wale kahenge, “Kya yeh woh log hain jo qasam khaate the...”The believers say, “Are these the ones who swore...”❓ False Oaths: Echoes Christian oath warnings (Matthew 5:33-37).
5:54يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا مَنْ يَرْتَدَّ مِنْكُمْ عَنْ دِينِهِ...Aye imaan walon, jo tum mein se apne deen se phir jaye...O you who believe, whoever among you turns back from his religion...⚠️ Apostasy: Similar to Christian backsliding (Hebrews 6:4-6).
5:55إِنَّمَا وَلِيُّكُمُ اللَّهُ وَرَسُولُهُ...Tumhara wali sirf Allah, uska Rasool aur imaan wale hain...Your ally is only Allah, His Messenger, and the believers...🤝 True Allies: Reflects Christian fellowship (1 John 1:7).
5:56وَمَنْ يَتَوَلَّ اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ...Jo Allah aur uske Rasool ko apna wali banaye...Whoever takes Allah and His Messenger as allies...🌟 Divine Support: Echoes Hindu divine protection (Bhagavad Gita 9:22).
5:57يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَتَّخِذُوا الَّذِينَ اتَّخَذُوا دِينَكُمْ هُزُوًا...Aye imaan walon, unhein dost na banao jo tumhare deen ka mazaaq udayein...O you who believe, do not take those who mock your religion as allies...🚫 Avoid Mockers: Similar to Christian “bad company” (1 Corinthians 15:33).
5:58وَإِذَا نَادَيْتُمْ إِلَى الصَّلَاةِ اتَّخَذُوهَا هُزُوًا...Aur jab tum namaz ke liye bulao toh woh isse mazaaq banate hain...When you call to prayer, they take it in mockery...😔 Mockery of Worship: Echoes Christian persecution (Matthew 5:11).
5:59قُلْ يَا أَهْلَ الْكِتَابِ هَلْ تَنْقِمُونَ مِنَّا...Kaho, “Aye Ahl-e-Kitaab, kya tum humse nafrat karte ho...”Say, “O People of the Book, do you resent us...”🤝 Interfaith Dialogue: Reflects Christian call to reason (Acts 17:2).
5:60قُلْ هَلْ أُنَبِّئُكُمْ بِشَرٍّ مِنْ ذَٰلِكَ مَثُوبَةً...Kaho, “Kya main tumhein isse bhi bura badla batayun...”Say, “Shall I inform you of worse than that as penalty...”⚖️ Divine Curse: Echoes biblical transformation curses (Deuteronomy 28:37).
5:61وَإِذَا جَاءُوكُمْ قَالُوا آمَنَّا وَقَدْ دَخَلُوا بِالْكُفْرِ...Jab woh tumhare paas aate hain toh kehte hain, “Hum imaan laaye,” jabki woh kufr ke saath aaye...When they come to you, they say, “We believe,” but they entered with disbelief...🎭 Hypocrisy: Similar to Christian false disciples (Matthew 7:15).
5:62وَتَرَىٰ كَثِيرًا مِنْهُمْ يُسَارِعُونَ فِي الْإِثْمِ...Tu dekhega unmein se zyadatar gunaah mein jaldi karte hain...You see many of them hastening into sin...🚫 Sinful Haste: Echoes Christian “broad path” (Matthew 7:13).
5:63لَوْلَا يَنْهَاهُمُ الرَّبَّانِيُّونَ وَالْأَحْبَارُ...Kyun unke rabbani aur alim unhein nahin rokte...Why do their rabbis and scholars not forbid them...🧑‍🏫 Scholarly Duty: Similar to Jewish prophetic rebuke (Isaiah 1:16-17).
5:64وَقَالَتِ الْيَهُودُ يَدُ اللَّهِ مَغْلُولَةٌ...Yahud ne kaha, “Allah ka haath bandha hua hai...”The Jews say, “Allah’s hand is chained...”🚫 False Claim: Refutes limiting God, akin to Christian omnipotence (Matthew 19:26).
5:65وَلَوْ أَنَّ أَهْلَ الْكِتَابِ آمَنُوا وَاتَّقَوْا...Agar Ahl-e-Kitaab imaan laaye aur taqwa ikhtiyar kiya...If the People of the Book had believed and feared Allah...🌟 Faith’s Reward: Echoes Hindu Bhakti rewards.
5:66وَلَوْ أَنَّهُمْ أَقَامُوا التَّوْرَاةَ وَالْإِنْجِيلَ...Agar woh Taurat aur Injeel ko qaaim karte...If they had upheld the Torah and the Gospel...📜 Scriptural Adherence: Similar to Christian obedience to scripture (John 14:15).
5:67يَا أَيُّهَا الرَّسُولُ بَلِّغْ مَا أُنْزِلَ إِلَيْكَ...Aye Rasool, jo tujh par naazil kiya gaya usse pahuncha de...O Messenger, proclaim what has been revealed to you...📢 Prophetic Duty: Echoes Christian Great Commission (Matthew 28:19).
5:68قُلْ يَا أَهْلَ الْكِتَابِ لَسْتُمْ عَلَىٰ شَيْءٍ...Kaho, “Aye Ahl-e-Kitaab, tum kisi cheez par nahin ho...”Say, “O People of the Book, you are [standing] on nothing...”📜 Scriptural Foundation: Reflects Christian “house on rock” (Matthew 7:24).
5:69إِنَّ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَالَّذِينَ هَادُوا وَالصَّابِئِينَ...Jo imaan laaye aur Yahudi, Sabai aur Nasara...Those who believe, the Jews, the Sabians, and the Christians...🤝 Universal Salvation: Contextual; parallels Christian inclusivity (Romans 10:12).
5:70لَقَدْ أَخَذْنَا مِيثَاقَ بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ...Humne Bani Israeel se ahd liya...We took a covenant from the Children of Israel...🤝 Israel’s Covenant: Echoes Exodus 24 (Judaism).
5:71وَحَسِبُوا أَلَّا تَكُونَ فِتْنَةٌ...Aur unhone socha ki koi aazmaish na hogi...They thought there would be no trial...⚖️ False Security: Similar to Christian complacency warning (Luke 12:19).
5:72لَقَدْ كَفَرَ الَّذِينَ قَالُوا إِنَّ اللَّهَ هُوَ الْمَسِيحُ...Kafir hue jo bole ki Allah Masih hai...They disbelieve who say, “Allah is the Messiah...”☝️ Monotheism: Rejects Christian Trinity, aligns with Jewish Shema (Deuteronomy 6:4).
5:73لَقَدْ كَفَرَ الَّذِينَ قَالُوا إِنَّ اللَّهَ ثَالِثُ ثَلَاثَةٍ...Kafir hue jo bole ki Allah teen ka teesra hai...They disbelieve who say, “Allah is one of three...”🚫 Against Trinity: Differs from Christian doctrine (Matthew 28:19).
5:74أَفَلَا يَتُوبُونَ إِلَى اللَّهِ وَيَسْتَغْفِرُونَهُ...Kya woh Allah ki taraf tauba nahin karte aur maafi nahin maangte...Will they not repent to Allah and seek His forgiveness...🙏 Call to Repentance: Echoes Christian call to repent (Acts 3:19).
5:75مَا الْمَسِيحُ ابْنُ مَرْيَمَ إِلَّا رَسُولٌ...Masih ibn Maryam sirf ek Rasool hai...The Messiah, son of Mary, was only a messenger...✝️ Jesus as Messenger: Differs from Christian divinity (John 1:1).
5:76قُلْ أَتَعْبُدُونَ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ مَا لَا يَمْلِكُ...Kaho, “Kya tum Allah ke siwa uski ibadat karte ho jo...”Say, “Do you worship besides Allah that which...”🚫 Against Idolatry: Echoes Jewish Ten Commandments (Exodus 20:3).
5:77قُلْ يَا أَهْلَ الْكِتَابِ لَا تَغْلُوا فِي دِينِكُمْ...Kaho, “Aye Ahl-e-Kitaab, apne deen mein hadd se na guzro...”Say, “O People of the Book, do not exaggerate in your religion...”⚖️ Moderation: Corrects Christian excesses, aligns with Jewish monotheism.
5:78لُعِنَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا مِنْ بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ...Bani Israeel ke kafiron par laanat ki gayi...Those who disbelieved among the Children of Israel were cursed...⚖️ Divine Curse: Echoes Deuteronomy 28:15 (Judaism).
5:79كَانُوا لَا يَتَنَاهَوْنَ عَنْ مُنْكَرٍ...Woh ek doosre ko buraai se nahin rokte the...They did not forbid one another from wrongdoing...🚫 Moral Neglect: Similar to Christian communal correction (Matthew 18:15-17).
5:80تَرَىٰ كَثِيرًا مِنْهُمْ يَتَوَلَّوْنَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا...Tu dekhega unmein se zyadatar kafiron ko dost banate hain...You see many of them allying with disbelievers...🤝 Misplaced Alliances: Echoes Jewish warnings against idolatry (Isaiah 44:9-20).
5:81وَلَوْ كَانُوا يُؤْمِنُونَ بِاللَّهِ وَالنَّبِيِّ...Agar woh Allah aur Nabi par imaan laate...If they believed in Allah and the Prophet...🙏 True Faith: Similar to Christian faith requirement (John 3:16).
5:82لَتَجِدَنَّ أَقْرَبَهُمْ مَوَدَّةً لِلَّذِينَ آمَنُوا الَّذِينَ قَالُوا إِنَّا نَصَارَىٰ...Tu sabse zyada dosti mein Nasara ko paayega jo kehte hain, “Hum Nasara hain...”You will find the nearest in affection to the believers those who say, “We are Christians...”🤝 Christian Affinity: Unique, but reflects Christian love command (John 13:34).
5:83وَإِذَا سَمِعُوا مَا أُنْزِلَ إِلَى الرَّسُولِ...Jab woh sunte hain jo Rasool par naazil hua...When they hear what was revealed to the Messenger...😢 Emotional Response: Similar to Christian conversion (Acts 2:37).
5:84وَمَا لَنَا لَا نُؤْمِنُ بِاللَّهِ...Aur humein kya hua ki hum Allah par imaan na layein...Why should we not believe in Allah...🙏 Faith’s Logic: Echoes Christian reasoning (Romans 10:9).
5:85فَأَثَابَهُمُ اللَّهُ بِمَا قَالُوا جَنَّاتٍ...Allah ne unhein unke qaul ki wajah se Jannat se nawaza...Allah rewarded them for what they said with Gardens...🌴 Paradise Reward: Echoes Hindu Swarga (heaven).
5:86وَالَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا وَكَذَّبُوا بِآيَاتِنَا...Aur jo kafir hue aur hamari aayaton ko jhuthlaya...Those who disbelieve and deny Our signs...🔥 Punishment: Similar to Christian judgment (Revelation 20:15).
5:87يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تُحَرِّمُوا طَيِّبَاتِ...Aye imaan walon, paak cheezon ko haram na karo...O you who believe, do not forbid good things...🍽️ Moderation: Echoes Christian liberty (1 Timothy 4:4).
5:88وَكُلُوا مِمَّا رَزَقَكُمُ اللَّهُ حَلَالًا طَيِّبًا...Aur khao jo Allah ne tumhein halal aur paak diya...Eat what Allah has provided for you, lawful and good...🍴 Gratitude for Provision: Similar to Jewish blessings over food.
5:89لَا يُؤَاخِذُكُمُ اللَّهُ بِاللَّغْوِ فِي أَيْمَانِكُمْ...Allah tumhein be-maani qasmon par nahi pakadta...Allah does not hold you accountable for unintentional oaths...⚖️ Oath Integrity: Echoes Christian oath warnings (Matthew 5 concentração:33-37).
5:90يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِنَّمَا الْخَمْرُ وَالْمَيْسِرُ...Aye imaan walon, sharab, jua... haram hain...O you who believe, intoxicants, gambling... are abominations...🚫 Prohibitions: Similar to Buddhist precepts against intoxicants.
5:91إِنَّمَا يُرِيدُ الشَّيْطَانُ أَنْ يُوقِعَ بَيْنَكُمُ الْعَدَاوَةَ...Shaitan sirf yeh chahta hai ki tum mein dushmani daal de...Satan only wants to cause enmity among you...😈 Satan’s Plan: Echoes Christian devil’s schemes (Ephesians 6:11).
5:92وَأَطِيعُوا اللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُوا الرَّسُولَ...Allah aur Rasool ki itaat karo...Obey Allah and obey the Messenger...🙇 Obedience: Similar to Jewish adherence to Torah (Deuteronomy 28:1).
5:93لَيْسَ عَلَى الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ جُنَاحٌ...Jo imaan laaye aur nek kaam kiye un par koi gunah nahin...There is no blame on those who believe and do good...🌟 Faithful Freedom: Echoes Christian grace (Romans 6:14).
5:94يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَيَبْلُوَنَّكُمُ اللَّهُ...Aye imaan walon, Allah tumhein aazmayega...O you who believe, Allah will test you...🧪 Divine Test: Similar to trials of Job (Judaism/Christianity).
5:95يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَقْتُلُوا الصَّيْدَ...Aye imaan walon, ihram mein shikaar na qatl karo...O you who believe, do not kill game while in ihram...🚫 Hunting Rules: Contextual; parallels Jewish hunting laws (Leviticus 17:13).
5:96أُحِلَّ لَكُمْ صَيْدُ الْبَحْرِ وَطَعَامُهُ...Tumhare liye samundar ka shikaar aur uska khana halal hai...Lawful for you is the game of the sea and its food...🐟 Seafood Permission: Similar to Christian dietary freedom (Acts 10:15).
5:97جَعَلَ اللَّهُ الْكَعْبَةَ الْبَيْتَ الْحَرَامَ...Allah ne Ka’ba, haram ghar ko qaaim kiya...Allah made the Ka’bah, the Sacred House...🕋 Sacred Ka’bah: Unique, but parallels Jewish Temple sanctity (1 Kings 8:13).
5:98اعْلَمُوا أَنَّ اللَّهَ شَدِيدُ الْعِقَابِ...Jaano ki Allah saza mein sakht hai...Know that Allah is severe in penalty...⚖️ Divine Justice: Echoes Christian judgment (Hebrews 10:31).
5:99مَا عَلَى الرَّسُولِ إِلَّا الْبَلَاغُ...Rasool par sirf paighaam pahunchana hai...The Messenger’s duty is only to convey...📢 Prophetic Role: Similar to Christian preaching (Mark 16:15).
5:100قُلْ لَا يَسْتَوِي الْخَبِيثُ وَالطَّيِّبُ...Kaho, “Bura aur paak barabar nahin...”Say, “The evil and the good are not equal...”⚖️ Moral Distinction: Echoes Christian purity (1 John 3:3).
5:101يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَسْأَلُوا عَنْ أَشْيَاءَ...Aye imaan walon, aisi cheezon ke baare mein na poochho...O you who believe, do not ask about things...❓ Avoid Unnecessary Questions: Similar to Christian “avoid foolish controversies” (Titus 3:9).
5:102قَدْ سَأَلَهَا قَوْمٌ مِنْ قَبْلِكُمْ ثُمَّ أَصْبَحُوا بِهَا كَافِرِينَ...Tumse pehle ek qoum ne yeh sawal kiya phir woh uske wajah se kafir ban gaye...A people before you asked about them, then became disbelievers...⚠️ Lesson from Past: Echoes Jewish questioning God (Exodus 17:2).
5:103مَا جَعَلَ اللَّهُ مِنْ بَحِيرَةٍ وَلَا سَائِبَةٍ...Allah ne koi bahira, sa’iba ya wasila muqarrar nahin kiya...Allah has not appointed a bahira, sa’iba, or wasila...🚫 Reject Superstitions: Similar to Christian rejection of paganism (Acts 19:19).
5:104وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمْ تَعَالَوْا إِلَىٰ مَا أَنْزَلَ اللَّهُ...Jab un se kaha jata hai ki Allah ke naazil kiye hue ki taraf aao...When it is said to them, “Come to what Allah has revealed...”📜 Divine Guidance: Echoes Christian call to scripture (2 Timothy 3:16).
5:105يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا عَلَيْكُمْ أَنْفُسَكُمْ...Aye imaan walon, apni jaan ki zimmedari tum par hai...O you who believe, upon you is [responsibility for] yourselves...🧠 Personal Accountability: Similar to Hindu Swadharma (self-duty).
5:106يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا شَهَادَةُ بَيْنِكُمْ إِذَا حَضَرَ أَحَدَكُمُ الْمَوْتُ...Aye imaan walon, jab tum mein se kisi ke paas maut aaye toh gawahi...O you who believe, testimony among you when death approaches...⚖️ Will Testimony: Similar to Jewish inheritance laws (Numbers 27:8-11).
5:107فَإِنْ عُثِرَ عَلَىٰ أَنَّهُمَا اسْتَحَقَّا إِثْمًا...Agar maloom ho ki dono ne gunaah kiya...If it is discovered that the two have committed sin...⚖️ False Testimony: Echoes Christian truthfulness (Ephesians 4:25).
5:108ذَٰلِكَ أَدْنَىٰ أَنْ يَأْتُوا بِالشَّهَادَةِ عَلَىٰ وَجْهِهَا...Yeh zyada qareeb hai ki woh gawahi ko uski haqeeqat par layein...That is more likely to bring true testimony...⚖️ Honest Witness: Similar to Jewish “do not bear false witness” (Exodus 20:16).
5:109يَوْمَ يَجْمَعُ اللَّهُ الرُّسُلَ...Jis din Allah Rasoolon ko jama karega...On the Day when Allah gathers the messengers...⚖️ Prophets’ Accountability: Unique, but parallels Christian judgment (Revelation 20:12).
5:110إِذْ قَالَ اللَّهُ يَا عِيسَى ابْنَ مَرْيَمَ اذْكُرْ نِعْمَتِي...Jab Allah ne kaha, “Aye Isa ibn Maryam, meri ne’mat yaad kar...”When Allah said, “O Jesus, son of Mary, remember My favor...”✝️ Jesus’ Miracles: Echoes Christian Gospel accounts (John 2:11).
5:111وَإِذْ أَوْحَيْتُ إِلَى الْحَوَارِيِّينَ...Aur jab maine Hawariyyin ke dil mein daala...When I inspired the disciples...🕊️ Disciples’ Faith: Similar to Christian apostles’ calling (Acts 1:8).
5:112إِذْ قَالَ الْحَوَارِيُّونَ يَا عِيسَى ابْنَ مَرْيَمَ...Jab Hawariyyin ne kaha, “Aye Isa ibn Maryam...”When the disciples said, “O Jesus, son of Mary...”🍽️ Table from Heaven: Parallels Christian Last Supper (Luke 22:19), with differences.
5:113قَالُوا نُرِيدُ أَنْ نَأْكُلَ مِنْهَا وَتَطْمَئِنَّ قُلُوبُنَا...Unhone kaha, “Hum isse khana chahte hain aur hamare dil mutma’in ho...”They said, “We wish to eat from it and let our hearts be reassured...”🙏 Sign for Faith: Similar to Christian seeking signs (John 6:30).
5:114قَالَ عِيسَى ابْنُ مَرْيَمَ اللَّهُمَّ رَبَّنَا أَنْزِلْ عَلَيْنَا مَائِدَةً...Isa ibn Maryam ne kaha, “Aye Allah, hamare Rab, hum par ma’ida naazil kar...”Jesus, son of Mary, said, “O Allah, our Lord, send down a table...”🍽️ Prayer for Miracle: Echoes Christian prayer for provision (Matthew 6:11).
5:115قَالَ اللَّهُ إِنِّي مُنَزِّلُهَا عَلَيْكُمْ...Allah ne kaha, “Main isse tum par naazil karne wala hoon...”Allah said, “I will send it down to you...”🕊️ Divine Response: Similar to Christian answered prayer (John 16:24).
5:116وَإِذْ قَالَ اللَّهُ يَا عِيسَى ابْنَ مَرْيَمَ أَأَنْتَ قُلْتَ...Jab Allah ne kaha, “Aye Isa ibn Maryam, kya tune kaha...”When Allah said, “O Jesus, son of Mary, did you say...”☝️ Jesus’ Denial of Divinity: Differs from Christian doctrine (John 10:30).
5:117مَا قُلْتُ لَهُمْ إِلَّا مَا أَمَرْتَنِي بِهِ...Maine un se sirf wahi kaha jo tune mujhe hukm diya...I said to them only what You commanded me...🙏 Jesus’ Submission: Aligns with Jewish prophetic obedience (Isaiah 6:8).
5:118إِنْ تُعَذِّبْهُمْ فَإِنَّهُمْ عِبَادُكَ...Agar tu unhein saza de toh woh tere bande hain...If You punish them, they are Your servants...⚖️ Divine Mercy Plea: Echoes Christian intercession (1 Timothy 2:1).
5:119قَالَ اللَّهُ هَٰذَا يَوْمُ يَنْفَعُ الصَّادِقِينَ صِدْقُهُمْ...Allah ne kaha, “Yeh woh din hai jab sachchon ka sach unhein faida dega...”Allah said, “This is the Day when the truthful benefit from their truth...”🌟 Truth’s Reward: Similar to Hindu Satya (truthfulness).
5:120لِلَّهِ مُلْكُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ...Allah ke liye aasmanon aur zameen ka mulk hai...To Allah belongs the dominion of the heavens and the earth...🌍 Divine Sovereignty: Echoes Christian “Thine is the kingdom” (Matthew 6:13).
